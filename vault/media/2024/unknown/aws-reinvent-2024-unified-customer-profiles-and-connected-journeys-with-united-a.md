# Unified Customer Profiles and Connected Journeys with United Airlines

## Building a 360-Degree View of the Customer

United Airlines partnered with AWS to transform how the company understands and engages with its customers by consolidating data from numerous disparate touchpoints into a single, unified profile. By leveraging AWS Entity Resolution and AWS Clean Rooms, United addresses one of the most persistent challenges facing large enterprises: reconciling fragmented customer records that span booking systems, loyalty programs, mobile applications, in-flight experiences, and marketing channels. This consolidated view enables the airline to recognize each traveler consistently across every interaction, regardless of the channel or device used.

The migration of on-premises workloads to AWS served as the foundation for this transformation. By adopting a modern data architecture in the cloud, United gained the elasticity and processing power needed to handle highly distributed datasets at scale, while simultaneously reducing the operational burden associated with legacy infrastructure. The result is a flexible analytics environment capable of generating timely business insights that were previously difficult or impossible to extract.

## Personalization, Privacy, and Partnerships

### Delivering Connected Journeys

With a unified customer profile in place, United applies these insights to deliver more personalized experiences throughout the traveler's journey. The airline can tailor communications, optimize inventory utilization, and craft offers that align with individual preferences and behaviors. This personalization extends into United's media and marketing strategy, allowing the company to design connected campaigns that reach customers with relevant messaging at the right moments, ultimately driving stronger engagement and revenue growth.

### Maintaining Governance and Secure Collaboration

A central theme of United's approach is the rigorous data governance and security framework that underpins the entire solution. AWS Clean Rooms plays a critical role here, enabling United to collaborate with partners and media providers on shared datasets without exposing sensitive customer information. This secure collaboration model allows the airline to enrich its understanding of customers and amplify campaign effectiveness while maintaining strict privacy controls and compliance with regulatory requirements. By combining identity resolution with privacy-preserving collaboration, United demonstrates how enterprises can unlock the value of customer data responsibly while sustaining trust with travelers and partners alike.

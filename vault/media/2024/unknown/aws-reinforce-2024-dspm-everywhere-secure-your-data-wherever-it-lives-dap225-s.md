# DSPM Everywhere: Securing Data Across Hybrid, Multicloud, and On-Premises Environments

## Overview of the Session

This AWS re:Inforce 2024 lightning talk, presented by Rubrik as an AWS Partner, addresses one of the most pressing challenges facing modern enterprises: knowing exactly where sensitive data resides and reducing the risk of exposure and exfiltration across increasingly fragmented digital environments. The session frames Data Security Posture Management (DSPM) as an essential discipline for organizations that must protect information distributed across hybrid infrastructures, multiple cloud providers, software-as-a-service applications, and traditional on-premises systems. As enterprise data continues to proliferate beyond the boundaries of any single platform, security teams need unified visibility and consistent controls to keep pace with evolving threats and regulatory requirements.

## The Case for Comprehensive Data Security Posture Management

### Discovering and Classifying Sensitive Data at Scale

A central theme of the presentation is the imperative to automatically discover and classify all sensitive, proprietary, and restricted data wherever it lives. Manual approaches to data inventory simply cannot keep up with the volume and velocity of data creation in today's enterprises, leaving blind spots that adversaries can exploit. The talk emphasizes that effective DSPM begins with continuous, automated discovery that spans AWS environments, SaaS platforms, and on-premises repositories alike. By classifying data based on sensitivity and business context, organizations gain the foundational awareness needed to apply appropriate protections, prioritize remediation efforts, and demonstrate compliance with data protection mandates.

### Reducing the Risk of Exfiltration and Cyber Attack Impact

Beyond discovery, the session highlights proactive measures that organizations can take to minimize both the likelihood and the consequences of data exfiltration. By understanding which data carries the greatest risk and where it is concentrated, security teams can tighten access controls, eliminate unnecessary copies, and reduce the overall attack surface. The presentation positions DSPM not merely as a visibility tool but as the basis for resilience, enabling enterprises to limit the blast radius of incidents and recover more quickly when breaches occur. This proactive posture is especially critical as ransomware and data extortion campaigns increasingly target sensitive information across distributed environments.

### Unified Protection Across Hybrid and Multicloud Architectures

The final theme centers on the importance of consistency. Rubrik's approach, as outlined in the talk, is to deliver DSPM capabilities that work uniformly whether data sits in AWS, in a SaaS application, or behind the corporate firewall. This unified model spares security teams from stitching together disparate point solutions and ensures that policies, classifications, and risk assessments remain coherent across the entire data estate. The outcome is a more defensible posture in which organizations can confidently answer where their sensitive data lives, who can access it, and how it is protected, regardless of the underlying platform.

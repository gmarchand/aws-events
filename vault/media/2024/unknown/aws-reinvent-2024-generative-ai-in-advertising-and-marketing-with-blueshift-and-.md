# Generative AI in Advertising and Marketing: Insights from Blueshift and VidMob

## Overview of Generative AI's Role in Modern Marketing

The advertising and marketing industries are undergoing a significant transformation driven by generative AI capabilities offered through AWS. This session at AWS re:Invent 2024 brought together two innovative companies, Blueshift and VidMob, to demonstrate how marketers and advertisers are leveraging AWS generative AI services to accelerate creative development, automate complex workflows, personalize customer experiences at scale, and optimize campaign performance. The discussion centered on practical, real-world applications that demonstrate measurable business value, particularly in improving return on ad spend (ROAS) and enhancing customer engagement through intelligent content delivery.

## Industry Applications and Strategic Value

### Personalization Through LLM Embeddings at Blueshift

Blueshift focuses on solving one of the most persistent challenges in marketing: delivering truly personalized experiences across diverse customer touchpoints. By harnessing large language model embeddings, Blueshift enhances its recommendation engine to understand content and customer behavior at a deeper semantic level. This approach allows the platform to maximize the value of existing content assets by intelligently matching them to the right audiences at the right moments. Rather than relying solely on traditional collaborative filtering or rule-based segmentation, the embedding-driven approach captures contextual nuance, leading to more relevant recommendations that drive higher engagement and conversion rates. The result is a marketing platform that helps brands extend the lifecycle and impact of their creative assets while delivering experiences that feel uniquely tailored to each customer.

### Creative Intelligence and Optimization at VidMob

VidMob takes a complementary approach by applying generative AI to the creative side of advertising. The company experiments with and fine-tunes generative AI models to extract actionable insights from creative assets, helping advertisers understand which visual, narrative, and structural elements drive performance. This creative intelligence enables marketers to make data-informed decisions about ad design, ultimately optimizing ROAS by identifying patterns that resonate with target audiences. By moving beyond intuition-based creative development toward AI-driven creative analytics, VidMob empowers brands to iterate faster, test more variations efficiently, and continuously refine their creative strategies based on performance signals surfaced by generative models.

### Outcomes and Forward-Looking Implications

Together, these case studies illustrate a broader shift in how the marketing technology ecosystem is integrating generative AI. The combination of personalized content distribution and AI-driven creative optimization represents an end-to-end transformation of the advertising value chain. Marketers gain the ability to scale personalization without sacrificing relevance, while creative teams receive granular feedback that improves both efficiency and effectiveness. AWS provides the underlying infrastructure and AI services that make these innovations possible, allowing companies like Blueshift and VidMob to experiment, fine-tune, and deploy sophisticated models in production. The session underscored that generative AI is no longer experimental in marketing—it is becoming foundational technology that reshapes how brands connect with consumers and measure success.

# Optimized Migration Journeys to AMD: Lessons from Pixar and Epic

## Introduction to AMD-Powered Migration on AWS

This AWS re:Invent 2024 session, presented in partnership with AMD, explores how two leading organizations—Pixar Animation Studios and Epic Systems, a major healthcare software provider—evolved their cloud strategies beyond simple "lift and shift" migrations. Both organizations leveraged AMD EPYC-powered Amazon EC2 instances to optimize cost, performance, and energy efficiency while achieving the elasticity needed for cloud-scale operations. The session emphasizes that meaningful cloud transformation requires deliberate optimization rather than mere infrastructure replication, and it highlights the broader AMD ecosystem of partners and tools available to support enterprises throughout their migration journeys.

## Customer Success Stories

### Pixar's Cloud Rendering Transformation

Pixar shared how its transition to AMD EPYC-based EC2 instances reshaped the way artists produce visual content. By moving rendering workloads to the cloud, Pixar achieved a 30% faster runtime, enabling its RenderMan art challenges to deliver stunning visuals on tighter timelines. The cloud-based approach gave artists access to virtually unlimited compute capacity on demand, removing the bottlenecks traditionally associated with on-premises render farms. This shift allowed creative teams to iterate more freely, experiment with more complex scenes, and ultimately elevate the quality of their output while keeping infrastructure costs under control.

### Epic's Healthcare SaaS Expansion

Epic, a cornerstone provider of electronic health record systems, used AMD EPYC instances to boost ECP (Epic Capacity Planning) sizing by 36%, significantly improving the efficiency of its workloads. This performance gain directly supported Epic's expansion of SaaS cloud capabilities, enabling the company to serve healthcare customers at greater scale while maintaining the reliability and responsiveness that clinical environments demand. The migration demonstrated that even highly regulated, mission-critical healthcare applications can realize substantial performance and cost benefits by selecting the right underlying compute platform.

### The AMD Ecosystem and Optimization Approach

Beyond the customer stories, the session underscored AMD's robust ecosystem of partners and assessment tools designed to help organizations evaluate their existing applications, identify optimization opportunities, and migrate infrastructure in ways that eliminate unnecessary spending. The overarching message is that customers achieve the greatest value when they pair migration with right-sizing, instance selection, and workload tuning—turning a routine infrastructure move into a strategic improvement in cost, performance, and sustainability.

## Key Takeaways

The combined experiences of Pixar and Epic illustrate that AMD EPYC-powered EC2 instances can deliver tangible benefits across vastly different industries, from creative media production to healthcare SaaS. Organizations that move beyond lift-and-shift to embrace true optimization stand to gain measurable improvements in runtime performance, capacity efficiency, and energy use. With AMD's partner ecosystem and AWS's global infrastructure, enterprises have a clear pathway to modernize workloads while reducing operational costs and supporting sustainability goals.

# Dentsu and AWS: Making Literature Accessible Through "Easy Reading"

## Bridging the Accessibility Gap with AI

Dentsu Creative, in collaboration with AWS, has introduced a groundbreaking artificial intelligence tool called "Easy Reading," designed to address a long-standing accessibility challenge faced by millions of people worldwide who live with intellectual and developmental disabilities. The tool tackles a fundamental issue: most written content, from novels to everyday information, is presented in formats that are inaccessible to individuals who struggle with complex language structures. By harnessing the power of AWS infrastructure and AI capabilities, Dentsu has created a scalable solution capable of transforming any piece of text into the internationally recognized Easy-to-Read format, a standardized approach that simplifies vocabulary, sentence structure, and presentation to make content comprehensible to a far broader audience.

## Transforming Literary Heritage into an Inclusive Experience

### The Visit Portugal Partnership

To demonstrate the real-world impact of Easy Reading, Dentsu has partnered with Visit Portugal on an initiative that reframes how people can experience a country's culture. Rather than relying solely on traditional tourism, the partnership invites people to "visit" Portugal through its literary heritage. Three renowned classics of Portuguese literature have been adapted using the Easy Reading tool and made freely available on easyreadingportugal.com, opening these culturally significant works to readers who would otherwise be excluded from engaging with them. This approach reframes accessibility not as a compliance exercise but as an invitation to participate fully in cultural life.

### Launch and Promotion

The initiative will be formally celebrated in Lisbon in April, accompanied by a coordinated promotional campaign rolled out across Visit Portugal's digital channels. The campaign aims to drive awareness of the platform, encourage adoption among the disability community and their families, and showcase how AI built on AWS can deliver tangible social impact. By pairing a national tourism organization with a creative agency and cloud technology, the project establishes a replicable model for how cultural institutions can extend their reach to historically underserved audiences.

## Outcomes and Broader Implications

The Easy Reading initiative illustrates how generative AI, when paired with thoughtful partnerships and clear social purpose, can deliver measurable inclusion outcomes at scale. The collaboration between Dentsu Creative, Visit Portugal, and AWS demonstrates that accessibility tools no longer need to rely on slow, manual adaptation processes; instead, AI-driven translation into Easy-to-Read formats can unlock vast libraries of existing content almost instantly. The project positions Easy Reading as a foundation that other organizations, governments, and publishers could adopt to make their own content accessible, suggesting a future in which intellectual disability is no longer a barrier to engaging with literature, information, or cultural heritage.

# Amazon MGM Studios Automates Financial Close with SAP and AWS

## Overview of the Transformation

Amazon MGM Studios undertook a significant modernization of its SAP financial close process by integrating AWS services directly into its ERP environment. The financial close, traditionally a labor-intensive cycle requiring extensive data manipulation, validation, and reporting, became a prime candidate for automation. By leveraging the AWS SDK for SAP ABAP alongside complementary analytics tools, the studio reimagined how its finance teams handle recurring monthly and quarterly close activities, ultimately accelerating delivery while improving accuracy.

## Technical Approach and Implementation

### Bridging SAP and AWS with the ABAP SDK

At the heart of the solution is the AWS SDK for SAP ABAP, which allows developers to invoke AWS services natively from within SAP using only a few lines of ABAP code. Amazon MGM Studios used this capability to eliminate manual data extraction and transformation steps that previously consumed significant time during each close cycle. Rather than exporting data, manipulating it externally, and reimporting results, the team embedded calls to AWS services directly into their SAP workflows. This tight integration removed friction, reduced opportunities for error, and allowed automation to run consistently as part of the standard close process.

### Reporting and Analytics with Amazon QuickSight

To complement the automation layer, the studio adopted Amazon QuickSight as the business intelligence engine for validation, reconciliation checks, and audit reporting. QuickSight dashboards gave finance and audit stakeholders timely visibility into close metrics, enabling them to identify discrepancies earlier and validate results with greater confidence. This shift moved reporting from static, after-the-fact deliverables to interactive, near-real-time insights that support continuous review throughout the close period.

## Business Outcomes and Broader Implications

### Faster, More Accurate Close Cycles

The combined automation and analytics approach delivered measurable improvements for Amazon MGM Studios. Reporting accuracy increased as manual touchpoints were removed, productivity rose as finance professionals shifted away from repetitive data wrangling, and the overall close cycle shortened, allowing the team to call "that's a wrap" on each period sooner. These gains free finance staff to focus on analysis and strategic insight rather than mechanical processing.

### A Pattern for Other SAP Customers

The session positions this work as a replicable blueprint for other organizations running SAP. With more than 200 AWS services accessible through the ABAP SDK, customers can extend SAP processes well beyond financial close—into areas such as machine learning, document processing, storage, and event-driven integration—without leaving their familiar SAP development environment. Amazon MGM Studios' experience demonstrates that meaningful transformation can be achieved incrementally, using small amounts of code to unlock substantial operational value.

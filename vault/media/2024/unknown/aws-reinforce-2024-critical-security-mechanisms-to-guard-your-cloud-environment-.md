# Critical Security Mechanisms to Guard Your Cloud Environment

## Overview of the Session

This AWS re:Inforce 2024 lightning talk, delivered by Fortra as an AWS Partner, addresses the growing need for proactive security measures to protect modern cloud environments. The session centers on a core premise: as organizations migrate critical applications, APIs, and data to the cloud, traditional reactive security approaches fall short. Defenders must instead anticipate threats and embed protection across the full stack of cloud-native assets. Fortra positions itself as a cybersecurity ally that combines deep domain expertise with AWS-integrated tooling to help customers safeguard what matters most to their business.

## Key Themes and Security Mechanisms

### Proactive Protection Across Applications, APIs, and Data

The presentation emphasizes that applications, APIs, and data form the three primary attack surfaces in the cloud and require coordinated defenses rather than siloed point solutions. Fortra advocates for a layered approach in which advanced email security guards against phishing and business email compromise, the most common initial access vectors, while cloud-native data protection ensures sensitive information remains controlled regardless of where it travels. By unifying these capabilities, organizations can reduce the gaps that adversaries typically exploit when transitioning between perimeter, identity, and data layers.

### Leveraging AWS Threat Intelligence and Partner Expertise

A central message of the talk is the value of pairing Fortra's security portfolio with AWS-native threat intelligence to detect and respond to emerging threats more quickly. This integration enables customers to enrich detections with contextual signals from across the AWS ecosystem, accelerating incident response and improving accuracy. The collaboration between Fortra and AWS reflects a broader industry shift toward partner-delivered security mechanisms that extend AWS's shared responsibility model, allowing customers to focus on their business while trusted specialists handle increasingly sophisticated threat landscapes.

### Outcomes and Strategic Takeaways

The overarching outcome Fortra promotes is resilience: organizations that adopt advanced email security, cloud-native data protection, and AWS-integrated threat intelligence are better positioned to defend, detect, and recover from attacks. Attendees are encouraged to evaluate their current posture against these critical mechanisms, identify coverage gaps, and consider partner-led solutions to fill them. The session closes by reinforcing that security in the cloud is a continuous discipline, requiring both the right technology and the right expertise to keep pace with evolving adversaries.

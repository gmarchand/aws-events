# AWS for Media & Entertainment at NAB 2023

## Overview of AWS's Presence at NAB 2023

At the 2023 NAB Show, Amazon Web Services showcased its commitment to the media and entertainment (M&E) industry by presenting the most comprehensive and purpose-built cloud capabilities tailored to this sector. AWS positioned itself as a transformative partner for content creators, broadcasters, and distributors, emphasizing how its cloud services empower organizations to create, deliver, and monetize content more efficiently than ever before. The company's appearance at NAB underscored its ongoing investment in helping media businesses adapt to evolving consumer demands and shifting industry dynamics.

## Six Purpose-Built Solution Areas

AWS structures its media and entertainment offerings around six core solution areas that collectively address the entire content lifecycle. Content Production solutions enable studios and creators to leverage cloud-based tools for everything from pre-production planning to post-production workflows. Media Supply Chain and Archive services help organizations manage, store, and retrieve massive volumes of content efficiently while ensuring long-term preservation. Broadcast and Live Production capabilities allow traditional broadcasters and live event producers to migrate workflows to the cloud, gaining flexibility and scalability without sacrificing reliability.

Beyond production, AWS supports Direct-to-Consumer and Streaming services that enable media companies to launch and operate over-the-top platforms at global scale. Data Science and Analytics tools provide actionable insights from viewer behavior and operational data, while Monetization solutions help businesses unlock new revenue streams through advertising, subscriptions, and other commerce models.

### Accelerating Time to Value

A central theme of AWS's NAB 2023 presentation was the ability for customers to select the right combination of tools and partners for their specific workloads. By leveraging AWS's extensive partner ecosystem alongside native cloud services, media companies can accelerate production launches and achieve faster time to value. This flexibility allows organizations of all sizes—from independent creators to global media conglomerates—to build customized solutions that meet their unique business needs while transforming traditional workflows into agile, cloud-native operations that drive innovation across the entire content value chain.

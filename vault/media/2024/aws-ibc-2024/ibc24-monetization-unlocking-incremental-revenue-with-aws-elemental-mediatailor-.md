# Unlocking Incremental Revenue with AWS Elemental MediaTailor and Amazon Ads

## Overview of the Workflow Solution

At IBC24, AWS presented a workflow demonstration designed to help publishers unlock unique and incremental advertising demand for their ad-supported streaming television and audio content. The solution combines AWS Elemental MediaTailor with Amazon Ads infrastructure to address two persistent challenges faced by streaming publishers: improving fill rates and increasing effective CPMs. By bringing together server-side ad insertion technology with premium demand sources, the workflow enables publishers to monetize their inventory more effectively while maintaining a seamless viewer experience.

The integrated approach is built specifically for customers who already rely on Google Ad Manager as their primary ad server and programmatic platform. Rather than requiring publishers to abandon their existing technology stack, the workflow layers Amazon Ads demand on top of GAM, allowing publishers to access additional revenue streams without disrupting established operations.

## Technical Architecture and Integration Points

### Server-Side Header Bidding and Player Instrumentation

The architecture leverages Amazon Publisher Services (APS) to provide server-side header bidding, which introduces competitive bidding from Amazon Demand Side Platform (ADSP) into the ad decisioning process before requests reach Google Ad Manager. This server-side approach reduces latency and improves the reliability of ad delivery for streaming environments where client-side bidding can introduce buffering or timing issues.

To ensure measurement transparency and viewability validation, the workflow integrates the Datazoom player SDK, which delivers IAB-certified Open Measurement support. This certification gives advertisers confidence that impressions are being measured according to industry standards, which in turn supports higher CPMs and stronger demand from premium brands.

### Demand Sources and Publisher Tooling

Demand flowing through the workflow includes both Amazon's owned brands and premium third-party advertisers accessible through Amazon DSP, expanding the buyer pool beyond what publishers typically reach through GAM alone. Beyond raw demand, publishers gain access to the broader APS Enterprise toolset, including Deal Cloud for private marketplace transactions, Amazon Publisher Cloud for audience activation, Connections Marketplace for partner integrations, and Amazon Measurement and Insights for performance analytics.

## Business Outcomes for Publishers

The combined workflow delivers measurable improvements in monetization performance by increasing competition for each ad opportunity. Higher fill rates ensure that more available impressions are successfully monetized, while the introduction of premium Amazon Ads demand drives up the prices publishers receive. By unifying these capabilities into a single workflow built on AWS Elemental MediaTailor, publishers can capture incremental revenue from streaming TV and audio inventory without rebuilding their ad infrastructure, making the solution particularly attractive for established media companies seeking to maximize the value of their existing audiences.

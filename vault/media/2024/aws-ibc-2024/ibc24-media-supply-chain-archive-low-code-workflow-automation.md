# Low-Code Workflow Automation for Media Supply Chains

## Overview of the Demonstration

This IBC24 demonstration addresses a persistent challenge in the media industry: legacy content workflows tend to be rigid, slow to adapt, and burdensome to modify, leaving creative and editorial teams constrained by technical bottlenecks. The showcased solution introduces a low-code automation approach that empowers non-technical users to build and adjust custom applications themselves, freeing them to concentrate on creative and editorial outcomes rather than infrastructure concerns. By stitching together AWS services with a curated set of partner technologies, the demo illustrates how an end-to-end media supply chain can be reimagined to be both flexible and efficient.

## Architecture and Integrated Technologies

The solution unifies several specialized tools into a coherent workflow. Embrace's Pulse-IT serves as the low-code orchestration layer, allowing teams to design and deploy workflow logic without deep engineering expertise. Codemill's Accurate.Video provides advanced quality control capabilities, ensuring that assets meet technical and editorial standards before progressing downstream. Veritone's Digital Media Hub functions as the B2B distribution portal, enabling syndication of content to partners and customers. Underpinning content authenticity, the open C2PA standard is integrated to validate provenance, giving downstream consumers confidence in the origin and integrity of media assets.

### The Asset Lifecycle in Action

Walking through the demo, users follow an asset from ingest through distribution. Content first enters the advanced QC stage in Accurate.Video, where automated and human-assisted checks confirm quality. Authentication using C2PA then attaches verifiable provenance metadata to the asset, establishing a tamper-evident chain of custody. Pulse-IT coordinates the transitions between stages, applying business rules and routing decisions defined by non-technical users. Once approved, the asset is syndicated through Veritone's Digital Media Hub for multichannel B2B distribution, completing the journey from raw content to monetizable, audience-ready output.

## Outcomes and Business Value

By adopting this integrated low-code pattern, media organizations and content creators gain the ability to optimize their entire content lifecycle, from QC through provenance validation to multichannel distribution. The approach reduces dependence on engineering resources for workflow changes, accelerates time-to-market for new content offerings, and improves overall operational efficiency. Most importantly, it shifts the focus back to creative and editorial work, demonstrating how AWS services combined with specialized partner offerings can transform rigid legacy pipelines into agile, business-user-driven media supply chains.

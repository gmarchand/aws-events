# Streamlining Cloud-Based Post-Production Through Integrated Orchestration

## Overview of the Demonstration

At IBC24, a joint demonstration showcased how integrated orchestration and data management technologies can transform content production workflows in the cloud. The presentation centered on the combined capabilities of 7FiveFive's Lens management software and pixitmedia's ngenea data orchestration solution, illustrating how these tools work together to address the operational challenges faced by modern post-production teams. The demonstration emphasized that while cloud-based workflows already deliver significant efficiencies, the addition of purpose-built orchestration tools amplifies those benefits considerably.

## Operational Benefits of Production Orchestration

### Simplified Workflow Deployment and Creative Team Support

The core value proposition presented focuses on reducing the friction associated with deploying and managing post-production workflows in cloud environments. Lens, acting as the orchestration layer, allows production companies to spin up and configure complex workflows with greater ease, freeing technical teams from manual provisioning and configuration tasks. This streamlined deployment directly supports creative teams by ensuring they have rapid, reliable access to the tools and resources they need, when they need them. By removing infrastructure complexity from the creative process, the integrated solution enables artists and editors to remain focused on their craft rather than navigating technical bottlenecks.

### Intelligent Data Orchestration with ngenea

Underpinning the workflow layer is pixitmedia's ngenea, which handles the movement and placement of data across storage tiers and locations. This data orchestration capability ensures that media assets are positioned where they are needed for active work while remaining cost-effective when archived or idle. The integration between Lens and ngenea means that workflow decisions made at the orchestration level automatically trigger appropriate data movements, eliminating the manual handoffs that traditionally slow post-production pipelines.

### Improved Cost Visibility and Operational Metrics

A significant theme of the demonstration was the enhanced transparency that orchestration brings to cloud operations. Companies adopting the integrated solution gain clearer visibility into both the costs associated with their workflows and the operational metrics that indicate efficiency and performance. This visibility enables informed decision-making, helping production companies optimize spend, forecast budgets accurately, and identify opportunities for further efficiency gains. In an industry where cloud costs can escalate quickly without proper oversight, this level of insight represents a meaningful operational advantage.

## Key Takeaways

The demonstration ultimately positioned the combined Lens and ngenea offering as a practical answer to the challenges of modern, cloud-native post-production. By unifying workflow orchestration with intelligent data management, the solution delivers ease of deployment, stronger support for creative teams, and the financial and operational visibility that production companies increasingly require to run sustainable cloud operations.

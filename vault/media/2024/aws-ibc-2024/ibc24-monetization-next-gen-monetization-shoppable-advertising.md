# Next-Generation Monetization Through Shoppable Advertising

## Overview of the Demo Workflow

At IBC24, AWS presented a forward-looking demonstration of how media companies can transform their monetization strategies through shoppable advertising experiences. The workflow centers on delivering personalized, non-intrusive advertising that simultaneously maximizes revenue generation, enhances viewer engagement, and improves advertiser outcomes. By combining AWS Elemental MediaTailor's ad insertion capabilities with partner technology from InTheGame, the demonstration illustrates how content creators, streaming platforms, and advertisers can collaborate to deliver shoppable linear and non-linear ad formats at the scale required by large live sporting events.

This integrated approach addresses a long-standing tension in advertising-supported streaming: the need to generate revenue without disrupting the viewer experience. By making advertisements interactive and contextually relevant, the workflow turns traditional commercial breaks into opportunities for direct commerce and deeper audience engagement.

## Key Demonstrations and Capabilities

### Shoppable Advertising Powered by Generative AI

The first demonstration focuses on helping media publishers monetize their video content by transforming passive viewing into active commerce. This capability leverages generative AI alongside integrations with e-commerce shopping stores, enabling viewers to discover and purchase products that appear within the content they are watching. By embedding shopping opportunities directly into the streaming experience, publishers gain a new revenue stream while advertisers benefit from a far more measurable and conversion-oriented advertising channel. The use of generative AI streamlines the identification of products and the creation of relevant shopping prompts, reducing the manual effort traditionally associated with producing shoppable content.

### New Ad Formats with MediaTailor and InTheGame

The second demonstration showcases new advertising formats built on the combined strengths of AWS Elemental MediaTailor and InTheGame's advanced ad technology. Together, these tools deliver personalized, non-intrusive ad experiences tailored to individual viewers, particularly within the demanding environment of large-scale live sports. This pairing transforms monetization strategy by offering advertisers more sophisticated targeting and creative options, while preserving the immersive nature of live event viewing. The result is a measurable boost in viewer satisfaction alongside stronger advertising performance, demonstrating that scale, personalization, and viewer experience can coexist in modern streaming workloads.

## Outcomes for the Streaming Ecosystem

The combined demonstrations point to a clear direction for the future of streaming monetization. Media publishers gain new tools to extract value from their content libraries and live events, advertisers receive richer formats with stronger engagement signals, and viewers enjoy advertising that feels relevant rather than disruptive. By unifying AWS's media services with specialized partner technology, the workflow offers a practical blueprint for organizations seeking to evolve beyond traditional ad insertion toward interactive, commerce-driven experiences that scale with audience demand.

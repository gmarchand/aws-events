# Optimized FAST Channel Creation for Direct-to-Consumer Streaming

## The Growing Importance of FAST Channels and Personalization

Free Ad-Supported Streaming Television (FAST) has emerged as a critical battleground for direct-to-consumer streaming providers seeking to maximize viewer engagement and reduce churn. As the streaming landscape grows more competitive, platform operators face mounting pressure to deliver compelling linear-style experiences efficiently, drawing from their existing content libraries while tailoring programming to individual audience preferences. For FAST operators as well as first-party video OTT and mobile application providers, personalization of both content and the platform experience itself has become essential rather than optional, directly influencing watch time, advertising revenue, and long-term subscriber retention.

## Automating Channel Creation with ThinkAnalytics and AWS Elemental MediaTailor

AWS Partner ThinkAnalytics addresses the operational challenge of rapidly generating and managing FAST channels through its ThinkFAST solution, which leverages AWS Elemental MediaTailor's Channel Assembly capability. Rather than requiring manual scheduling and curation, ThinkFAST automatically transforms a platform's existing video on-demand assets into revenue-optimized FAST channel programming slates. The system continuously refines these schedules over time, applying user behavior data to improve content selection, sequencing, and monetization. This automation enables content owners to launch and operate more channels with less effort while ensuring that each channel evolves in response to actual audience consumption patterns, ultimately driving stronger advertising performance and viewer loyalty.

### Enhancing Video Quality with Intel Library for Video Super Resolution

Beyond scheduling and personalization, delivering an optimal viewing experience requires that legacy and lower-resolution content look its best on modern high-resolution devices. To that end, AWS Partners such as Intel contribute complementary technologies, including the Intel Library for Video Super Resolution (Intel Library for VSR), which upscales and optimizes video output quality for the highest-resolution playback environments. By combining automated channel creation, behavior-driven personalization, and advanced video quality enhancement, FAST operators can extract greater value from their existing content libraries while delivering a premium, differentiated experience to their direct-to-consumer audiences.

# Migrate to Monetize: Unlocking Value from Media Archives

## Overview of the Challenge and Solution

Media organizations frequently sit on enormous libraries of video, audio, and text-based content that represent significant intellectual property value, yet extracting monetary returns from these archives remains a persistent challenge. Legacy storage formats, fragmented metadata, and unclear rights ownership often prevent media owners from identifying and exploiting new revenue opportunities. The "Migrate to Monetize" demonstration showcased at IBC24 directly addresses this gap by presenting an end-to-end workflow that takes content from physical or legacy archives through to actively monetizable assets within a unified cloud environment.

The solution centers on AWS as the foundational platform and brings together a curated ecosystem of specialized partners, each contributing capabilities at a critical stage of the archive-to-revenue journey. By orchestrating migration, enrichment, rights management, and distribution within a single architecture, the approach transforms dormant archives into active commercial inventory.

## The Integrated Workflow and Partner Ecosystem

### Foundation on AWS and Media2Cloud Guidance

At the core of the demonstration sits the Guidance for Media2Cloud on AWS, a reference architecture that automates ingest, analysis, and management of large media libraries. Media2Cloud provides the backbone for moving content into AWS while applying machine learning services to generate technical metadata, transcripts, and content-aware tags. This guidance serves as the connective tissue across the partner stack, ensuring that assets ingested from disparate sources are normalized and made ready for downstream enrichment and commercialization.

### Migration and Metadata Enrichment

Cloudfirst contributes its Archive Migration as a Service offering, which handles the often complex task of physically and logically transferring content from legacy tape libraries, on-premises systems, and other archival formats into AWS storage. This service removes the operational burden of migration and ensures that content arrives in the cloud in a structured, accessible state. Once content lands in AWS, Vidispine Vidicore acts as the media asset management layer, providing the catalog, search, and workflow capabilities required to operate the archive at scale, while Vubiquity MetaVu enriches assets with deeper descriptive and editorial metadata that increases their discoverability and commercial appeal.

### Rights Intelligence and Monetization

A particularly novel element of the demonstration is PwC's AI-powered rights extraction capability, which analyzes contracts and supporting documentation to surface rights information that has historically been locked inside unstructured paperwork. This extracted intelligence flows into Rightsline, the rights management platform that governs how, where, and when content can be exploited. By combining automated rights discovery with formal rights administration, media owners gain a clear and enforceable view of what they can sell, license, or syndicate. The result is the ability to identify monetization opportunities that would otherwise remain hidden and to act on them with confidence that contractual obligations are respected.

## Outcomes and Strategic Value

The integrated demonstration delivers a coherent path from raw archive to revenue, illustrating how media owners can achieve intelligent content migration, enriched and searchable metadata, and well-governed rights-based monetization without assembling disparate tools themselves. The most important outcome is strategic: organizations can transition their archives from cost centers into active contributors to the business, opening targeted licensing, syndication, and direct distribution opportunities. By relying on AWS services in combination with proven partner solutions, media companies shorten time to value, reduce migration risk, and gain the analytical foundation needed to make informed decisions about which assets to invest in commercializing.

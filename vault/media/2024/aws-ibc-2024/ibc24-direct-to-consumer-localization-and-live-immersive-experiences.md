# Direct to Consumer: Localization and Live Immersive Experiences

## Reaching Global Audiences Through Cloud-Powered Personalization

Media companies are increasingly turning to cloud computing to expand their reach across global audiences while simultaneously tailoring content to match individual viewer preferences. The shift toward direct-to-consumer streaming has placed new demands on broadcasters and content providers, who must now deliver experiences that feel personal and engaging to viewers regardless of their location or language. Historically, achieving this level of personalization for live content presented substantial technical hurdles and prohibitive costs, particularly when it came to localizing live broadcasts with multi-language subtitles or offering viewers control over their viewing experience through multi-camera selection. AWS and its partner ecosystem are addressing these long-standing challenges by providing scalable, affordable solutions that transform how live content is produced, distributed, and consumed.

## Solving Live Localization at Scale

### Real-Time Transcription and Translation with SyncWords

AWS Partner SyncWords tackles the localization challenge by leveraging AWS services to deliver real-time transcription and translation for live video streams. This approach allows media companies to generate live subtitles in multiple languages simultaneously, breaking down language barriers that previously limited the global appeal of live content. By building on AWS infrastructure, SyncWords delivers this capability at a scale and price point that makes multi-language live streaming viable for a much wider range of content producers. Broadcasters can now confidently distribute live events to international audiences without the traditional overhead of dedicated human captioners for every target language, opening new revenue opportunities and audience segments that were previously out of reach.

## Creating Immersive Multi-View Experiences

### User-Selectable Camera Angles with Tiledmedia and AWS Elemental

The second major innovation discussed centers on giving viewers control over their own viewing experience through multi-view streaming. Using the Tiledmedia multiview video player in combination with AWS Elemental MediaLive and AWS Elemental MediaPackage, streaming platforms can combine multiple live encodings into a single multi-view package. This integrated approach allows viewers to choose their preferred camera angles in real time, transforming passive viewing into an interactive, immersive experience. Sports broadcasts, concerts, and other live events benefit particularly from this capability, as viewers can follow the action from the perspective that interests them most. By consolidating the workflow within AWS Elemental services, content providers avoid the complexity and cost traditionally associated with delivering synchronized multi-angle streams, making premium immersive experiences accessible to a broader market.

## Outcomes and Strategic Implications

The combined message of these solutions is that cloud-based architectures, supported by specialized AWS partners, are dismantling the technical and economic barriers that once limited live content innovation. Media companies adopting these approaches can simultaneously expand globally through automated localization and deepen viewer engagement through interactive, personalized experiences. The partnerships highlighted demonstrate a clear path forward in which scalability, affordability, and viewer-centric design converge, enabling direct-to-consumer streaming platforms to compete effectively in an increasingly crowded and globalized media landscape.

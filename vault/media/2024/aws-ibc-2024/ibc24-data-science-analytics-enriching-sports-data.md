# Enriching Sports Data with Generative AI on AWS

## Overview of the Demonstration

The Enriching Sports Data demonstration, presented at IBC 2024 within the Data Science & Analytics track, introduces a cost-effective and scalable solution for extracting meaningful insights from live sports content. The demonstration highlights how Generative AI, combined with a suite of AWS services, can transform raw broadcast feeds into structured, valuable data without requiring access to costly third-party data providers. This approach democratizes advanced analytics, making sophisticated sports data capabilities accessible to organizations that have traditionally been priced out of premium data feeds.

## Technical Architecture and AWS Services

The solution integrates several AWS services to deliver an end-to-end pipeline for sports content analysis. AWS Elemental MediaLive handles the ingestion and processing of live video feeds, while Amazon Transcribe converts commentary audio into searchable text. Amazon Bedrock provides the foundation models that power the generative AI capabilities, enabling the system to interpret and contextualize sports events. Amazon SageMaker rounds out the architecture by supporting custom machine learning workflows and model deployment. Together, these services form a cohesive pipeline that ingests live sports content, applies AI-driven analysis, and outputs enriched, structured data ready for downstream use.

### Identifying Actions, Players, and Outcomes

A core capability of the demonstration is its ability to automatically identify key moments in a match, recognize players involved, and capture the outcomes of specific actions. By analyzing both the visual content and the accompanying commentary, the system generates contextual metadata that mirrors the kind of insights typically produced by manual data collection teams or premium data providers.

## Target Audience and Business Value

The solution is specifically designed for tier 2 and tier 3 leagues and sports organizations, which often lack the budget to subscribe to expensive data feeds yet still need rich data to remain competitive in the modern sports landscape. By providing an alternative path to high-quality sports data, the demonstration enables these organizations to enhance fan engagement through more compelling content, support broadcasters with deeper storytelling material, and streamline back-office operations such as performance analysis and reporting.

### Outcomes and Strategic Impact

Ultimately, the demonstration positions Generative AI as a transformative tool for the sports industry, lowering the barrier to entry for data-driven decision making. Organizations adopting this approach can expect improved fan experiences, new opportunities for content creation, and operational efficiencies—all achieved through a scalable architecture built entirely on AWS.

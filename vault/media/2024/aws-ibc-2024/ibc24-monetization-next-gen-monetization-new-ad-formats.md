# Next-Generation Monetization Through Advanced Ad Formats

## Overview of the Demo Workflow

At IBC24, AWS presented a forward-looking demonstration of next-generation monetization strategies designed to address the evolving needs of content creators, streaming platforms, and advertisers. The showcase centered on a workflow that delivers personalized, non-intrusive advertising experiences while simultaneously maximizing revenue generation and improving viewer engagement. By prioritizing both advertiser outcomes and audience satisfaction, the workflow illustrates how modern ad technology can balance commercial objectives with user experience, a tension that has long challenged the streaming industry.

The core of the demonstration relies on AWS Elemental MediaTailor for server-side ad insertion, combined with the advanced ad technology capabilities of partner InTheGame. Together, these technologies enable shoppable linear and non-linear ad formats specifically tailored for large-scale live sporting events, where audience attention is high and monetization opportunities are time-sensitive.

## Shoppable Advertising Powered by Generative AI

### Transforming Video Content into Commerce Opportunities

The first demonstration highlights how shoppable advertising can help media publishers extract greater value from their video content. By leveraging generative AI alongside integrations with e-commerce shopping platforms, this approach transforms passive viewing into an interactive commercial experience. Viewers can engage directly with products featured in the content, allowing publishers to unlock new revenue streams beyond traditional advertising models. This integration of AI-driven personalization with retail infrastructure represents a significant evolution in how video content is monetized, blurring the line between entertainment and direct commerce.

## New Ad Formats with MediaTailor and InTheGame

### Personalized, Non-Intrusive Experiences for Live Sports

The second demonstration focuses on new ad formats developed through the combined capabilities of AWS Elemental MediaTailor and InTheGame's advanced ad technology. This integration enables ad experiences that are personalized to individual viewers while remaining non-intrusive, addressing a persistent industry challenge where aggressive advertising often degrades the viewing experience. Designed for the high-stakes environment of live sporting events, these formats transform monetization strategies by aligning advertiser goals with audience preferences. The result is a measurable boost in viewer satisfaction alongside improved advertising performance, demonstrating that thoughtful ad design can enhance rather than detract from the overall content experience.

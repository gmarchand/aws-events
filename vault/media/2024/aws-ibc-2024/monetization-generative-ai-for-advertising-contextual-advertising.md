# Generative AI for Advertising: Contextual Advertising on AWS

## Overview of the Solution

This demonstration showcases how AWS artificial intelligence and machine learning services can be leveraged to transform contextual advertising in the media and entertainment industry. By automatically extracting meaningful metadata from video content, the solution bridges the gap between publishers seeking to monetize their inventory and advertisers looking for contextually relevant placements. The approach addresses a long-standing challenge in digital advertising: ensuring that advertisements appear alongside content that resonates with their creative messaging and aligns with brand suitability guidelines.

## Technical Approach and Capabilities

### Automated Metadata Extraction and Taxonomy Mapping

At the core of the solution lies the use of AWS AI and ML services to analyze video content and extract relevant metadata in an automated fashion. Rather than relying on manual tagging or limited keyword-based approaches, the system intelligently identifies thematic elements, contextual signals, and content characteristics from the video itself. This extracted metadata is then mapped to advertising taxonomies, which can either be custom-built to suit a publisher's specific needs or aligned with widely adopted industry standards such as the Interactive Advertising Bureau (IAB) taxonomy or the Global Alliance for Responsible Media (GARM) framework. This flexibility ensures that the solution integrates seamlessly into existing advertising ecosystems while supporting tailored business requirements.

### Integration with OpenRTB Bid Requests

The content taxonomy mappings generated by the AI services are used to enrich and decorate OpenRTB bid requests, the standard protocol for real-time bidding in programmatic advertising. By embedding contextual metadata directly into the bid request, publishers provide advertisers, agencies, and brands with deeper insight into the nature of the inventory being offered. This enables more informed bidding decisions, allowing buyers to target content that is contextually similar to their ad creative or that adheres to their brand safety and suitability standards.

## Business Outcomes and Value

The solution delivers tangible value across the advertising value chain. Publishers can monetize their inventory more effectively by exposing rich contextual signals that command higher bids and attract premium advertisers. Advertisers, agencies, and brands benefit from improved campaign performance through better contextual alignment between their creatives and the surrounding content, while simultaneously mitigating brand safety risks. By automating metadata generation and taxonomy mapping at scale, the AWS-powered approach reduces manual overhead and unlocks new opportunities for contextual advertising in an era where privacy-conscious, content-aware targeting is increasingly critical.

# ADVisor: AI-Powered Scene and Ad Break Detection for Broadcasting

## Project Overview and Strategic Context

The ADVisor project represents a collaborative effort between Fincons Group and Mediaset to harness artificial intelligence for transforming video content management within a major broadcasting environment. Designed as a third-party solution built on top of the Guidance for Media2Cloud on AWS framework, ADVisor addresses the growing complexity of managing video assets across both linear television and over-the-top (OTT) distribution channels. By embedding AI directly into day-to-day operations, the project delivers Mediaset a measurable competitive advantage, streamlining established workflows while simultaneously opening new revenue streams that were previously difficult to access through manual processes alone.

## Core Capabilities and Technical Approach

### Automated Ad Break Identification

At the heart of ADVisor lies its ability to automatically detect optimal insertion points for advertising breaks within video content. Traditionally, this task requires significant manual effort from operators who must review footage scene by scene to identify natural transitions that preserve the viewer experience. ADVisor automates this analysis by applying scene detection algorithms that recognize narrative boundaries, dramatically reducing the time and labor involved while maintaining the editorial quality expected in professional broadcasting. The result is a faster, more consistent workflow that scales with the increasing volume of content Mediaset must process.

### Content Discovery Through Metadata Enrichment

Beyond ad break detection, ADVisor enhances content discoverability by generating comprehensive metadata and applying detailed tagging across video assets. This automated enrichment supports a wide range of downstream activities, from search and recommendation to archival management, enabling teams to locate, repurpose, and surface content far more efficiently than legacy cataloging methods would allow. The depth and accuracy of the generated metadata also lay the foundation for more advanced applications across the broadcaster's operations.

### Contextual Advertising and Monetization

By combining precise scene-level understanding with rich semantic tagging, ADVisor unlocks contextual advertising opportunities that align commercial messages with the content surrounding them. This capability extends Mediaset's monetization options beyond traditional time-based ad insertion, allowing advertisers to target moments and themes that resonate with their campaigns. The approach increases the value of inventory in both linear broadcasts and OTT streams, where audience expectations and competitive pressures demand more sophisticated, relevant advertising experiences.

## Outcomes and Business Impact

The ADVisor solution illustrates how applied AI can simultaneously reduce operational overhead and expand commercial possibilities. By automating tasks that historically consumed significant manual effort, Mediaset improves operational efficiency and frees skilled staff to focus on higher-value editorial decisions. At the same time, the enriched metadata layer and contextual advertising capabilities create new monetization pathways, positioning the broadcaster to compete more effectively in an increasingly fragmented media landscape. The collaboration between Fincons Group and Mediaset, leveraging AWS as the underlying cloud foundation, demonstrates a practical blueprint for broadcasters seeking to modernize content workflows and capture greater value from their video libraries.

# Generative AI for Advertising: Automated Ad Break Detection

## Overview of the Solution

This demonstration showcases an AWS-powered solution that automates the identification of optimal ad insertion points within pre-recorded video content. The system addresses a long-standing challenge in the broadcast and streaming industries: placing advertisements with frame-accurate precision in locations that feel natural to viewers and avoid disrupting the narrative flow of the content. By combining generative AI with traditional machine learning capabilities available on AWS, the solution removes much of the manual labor traditionally required of human editors while preserving the editorial oversight necessary for high-quality output.

## How the Technology Works

### Combining Generative AI with Traditional ML

The solution leverages AWS generative AI services alongside established AI and ML tools to analyze video content and detect contextual cues that indicate suitable ad break opportunities. Rather than relying on arbitrary timing or simple scene change detection, the system understands the storyline and identifies non-intrusive moments where advertising can be inserted without interrupting key narrative beats. This contextual awareness is what enables the placements to feel natural rather than disruptive to the viewing experience.

### Generating an Edit Decision List

The output of the analysis is an Edit Decision List (EDL), a standardized format compatible with most professional video editing software. The EDL contains the suggested ad break markers, allowing human editors to easily import the recommendations into their existing workflows. Editors then review the proposed insertion points, accept or refine them as needed, and finalize the placements before the markers are passed downstream.

### Integration with Downstream Distribution Systems

Once approved, the ad insertion instructions can be delivered to a wide range of downstream systems, including linear broadcast playout environments, FAST channel playout platforms, server-side ad stitching services, and client-side video players. This flexibility ensures that the solution fits into both traditional broadcast pipelines and modern streaming architectures.

## Outcomes and Value

The combination of automated detection and human editorial review delivers efficiency gains for content owners and broadcasters while maintaining quality control. Editors no longer need to manually scrub through long-form video to identify break points, and the resulting ad placements are more likely to preserve viewer engagement because they are chosen based on contextual analysis of the content. The approach ultimately supports stronger monetization of pre-recorded inventory across linear, FAST, and on-demand distribution channels.

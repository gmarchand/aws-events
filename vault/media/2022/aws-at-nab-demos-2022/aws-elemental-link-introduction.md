# AWS Elemental Link: Simplifying Live Video Ingestion

## Overview of the Service

AWS Elemental Link is a purpose-built encoding device that streamlines the process of connecting live video sources, such as cameras, to AWS Elemental MediaLive in the cloud. Designed to remove the complexity traditionally associated with broadcast-grade video contribution, the device handles the technical heavy lifting so that customers can focus on content rather than infrastructure. Once connected, the device automatically encodes the incoming video feed and reliably transmits it to AWS, where it can be processed, packaged, and distributed to viewers around the world.

## Device Options and Use Cases

AWS offers two variants of the Elemental Link device to accommodate different production requirements. The Link HD device targets workflows that depend on high-definition video, making it well suited for events, corporate streaming, sports broadcasts, and similar applications where standard broadcast quality is sufficient. For productions demanding higher fidelity, the Link UHD device delivers ultra-high-definition video, supporting more advanced use cases such as premium live entertainment and high-end broadcast scenarios. By offering tiered hardware, AWS allows organizations to match the device to the resolution and quality demands of their specific content.

### Setup and Remote Management

A defining feature of Elemental Link is its simplicity of deployment. Users only need to connect the device to a video source, an Ethernet network, and a power supply, after which it is ready to begin transmitting. There is no manual configuration of bitrates, codecs, or transport settings on the device itself. All control and monitoring occur through the AWS Management Console, enabling operators to manage devices from any location with internet access. This remote-first approach reduces on-site technical staffing requirements and gives broadcasters consistent visibility into device health and performance, regardless of where the camera or production is physically located. More information is available at the AWS Elemental MediaLive Link product page.

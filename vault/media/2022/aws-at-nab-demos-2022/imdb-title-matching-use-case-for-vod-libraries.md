# IMDb Title Matching for VOD Libraries

## Overview of the Use Case

Video-on-demand providers face a persistent challenge in maintaining rich, accurate, and engaging metadata across their content catalogs. As libraries grow into the thousands or even millions of titles, manually curating descriptive information for each piece of content becomes impractical, leading to inconsistent user experiences, reduced discoverability, and missed opportunities for personalization. This use case addresses how VOD platforms can systematically enrich their catalogs by leveraging authoritative third-party data sources, with IMDb serving as the industry benchmark for film and television metadata.

## Leveraging the IMDb Mojo Dataset

### Enriching VOD Catalogs Through Title Matching

The IMDb Mojo Dataset provides VOD providers with comprehensive, trusted metadata that can be matched against existing catalog entries to fill gaps and elevate the quality of content information. Through title matching, platforms align their internal titles with IMDb records, allowing them to inherit detailed attributes such as cast and crew credits, genres, ratings, plot summaries, release information, and box office performance. This enrichment transforms a basic catalog into a deeply descriptive content library that powers more sophisticated downstream applications.

### Driving Discovery, Personalization, and Business Outcomes

Once catalogs are enriched with IMDb metadata, VOD providers unlock significant improvements across the user experience. Enhanced metadata fuels more accurate search results, smarter recommendation engines, and richer content detail pages that help viewers make informed viewing decisions. Beyond consumer-facing benefits, this enriched data supports analytics, content acquisition strategies, and audience insights, enabling platforms to make data-driven decisions about programming and licensing. By integrating IMDb's authoritative dataset into their workflows, VOD operators can scale catalog quality without proportionally scaling manual curation efforts, resulting in stronger engagement, better retention, and a more competitive streaming offering.

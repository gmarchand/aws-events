# AWS M&E Demo: Content Production Dailies and Collaboration

## Overview of the Demonstration

At IBC 2022, AWS Media & Entertainment experts presented a demonstration focused on streamlining content production workflows through a cloud-based dailies review and approval system. The session highlighted how AWS services can be combined to support the fast-paced, collaborative nature of modern film and television production, where teams across multiple locations need timely access to footage captured on set.

The demonstration emphasized that dailies—the raw footage reviewed shortly after capture—remain a critical step in production. Traditionally, this process has been hindered by physical media handling, geographic distance between collaborators, and limited tools for structured feedback. AWS proposed a streamlined alternative that brings ingestion, processing, review, and approval into a unified cloud environment.

## Cloud-Based Dailies Review and Approval

### Workflow and Collaboration Capabilities

The featured solution allows production teams to ingest footage directly into AWS, where it is processed, transcoded into review-ready proxies, and made available to authorized stakeholders through a secure interface. Directors, producers, editors, and other approvers can view dailies remotely, leave time-coded notes, and approve or reject takes without needing to be physically co-located. This shift reduces turnaround time between shooting and creative decision-making, enabling production schedules to move forward more efficiently.

### Outcomes and Production Impact

By centralizing dailies in the cloud, the demonstrated system enhances collaboration across distributed crews, improves security through managed access controls, and reduces reliance on shipping physical drives or maintaining on-premises infrastructure. The overall outcome positions AWS as an enabler of more agile, globally coordinated production pipelines, where creative teams can focus on storytelling while the underlying technology handles the heavy lifting of media management, processing, and delivery.

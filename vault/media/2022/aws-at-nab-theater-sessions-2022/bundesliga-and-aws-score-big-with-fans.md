# Bundesliga and AWS: Transforming the Football Viewing Experience

## Strategic Partnership Driving Broadcast Innovation

The collaboration between Sportcast, the broadcast production arm responsible for Bundesliga's host coverage, and Amazon Web Services represents a significant leap forward in how professional football is delivered to global audiences. In a discussion between Tim Achberger, Head of Innovation and Technology at Sportcast, and Marc Aldrich, General Manager for Global Media and Entertainment at AWS, the two leaders outlined how cloud-powered analytics and next-generation production techniques are reshaping fan engagement. Their partnership has produced award-winning broadcast offerings that combine deep statistical insight with personalized viewing options, positioning the Bundesliga at the forefront of sports media innovation.

## Award-Winning Broadcast Offerings

### Tactical Feed and Interactive Feed

Sportcast has developed differentiated viewing experiences designed to serve diverse audience preferences. The tactical feed delivers a dedicated camera angle and analytical perspective tailored to viewers who want to study formations, player movement, and coaching strategy in detail, transforming match consumption into something closer to a coaching clinic. Complementing this, the interactive feed empowers fans to take greater control of their viewing experience, offering customizable elements that move beyond the traditional linear broadcast. Together, these feeds reflect a deliberate strategy to recognize that modern audiences are no longer satisfied with a single, one-size-fits-all production.

### Bundesliga Match Facts Powered by AWS

At the heart of the innovation is Bundesliga Match Facts powered by AWS, a suite of advanced statistics generated through machine learning and real-time data processing on the AWS cloud. These match facts surface insights such as expected goals, average positions, pressure metrics, and shot efficiency, providing both broadcasters and viewers with context that previously required hours of post-match analysis. By delivering these analytics live during matches, the partnership enriches commentary, deepens fan understanding, and creates compelling new storytelling tools for media partners worldwide.

## Outcomes and Future Direction

The discussion underscored that these offerings have already earned industry recognition while continuing to evolve. The combination of Sportcast's broadcast expertise with AWS's scalable cloud infrastructure and machine learning capabilities has enabled rapid iteration on new features, ensuring the Bundesliga remains competitive in a global sports media landscape where fan expectations continue to rise. The ongoing collaboration signals a sustained commitment to leveraging technology not as a novelty but as a core component of how the league connects with supporters, with further enhancements anticipated as both partners explore additional applications of data science and personalized media delivery.

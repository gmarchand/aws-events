# Agentic Interactive Live Viewing: Reimagining the Sports Fan Experience

## Overview of the Demo

Modern sports fans demand more than passive broadcasts. They expect interactive, dynamic, and personalized viewing experiences that sustain engagement throughout an event. This demo illustrates how broadcasters can meet those expectations by building a comprehensive fan engagement platform that unifies live streaming, real-time analytics, and contextual content generation. By combining AWS services with a curated set of AWS Partner solutions, the platform transforms a traditional broadcast into a living, responsive environment where viewers interact with the action as it happens, connect with fellow fans, and consume content tailored to their language preferences and viewing devices.

## Building Contextual Engagement with AI Agents

### Real-Time Quiz Generation Through Amazon Bedrock AgentCore

At the core of the experience is the use of AI agents powered by Amazon Bedrock AgentCore, which continuously analyze real-time sports data to identify moments worthy of fan interaction. Rather than relying on pre-scripted trivia, the system automatically generates quiz content that reflects what is unfolding on the field. The agents incorporate signals such as live match conditions, weather changes, evolving competition standings, and strategic decisions made by teams and coaches, producing contextually aware questions that feel timely and relevant. This agentic approach ensures that engagement opportunities arrive at the right moment, deepening the connection between the broadcast and the viewer.

### Community Interaction Powered by LiveLike and Tiledmedia

The platform integrates AWS Partner solutions from Tiledmedia and LiveLike to deliver the streaming foundation and the interactive layer that surrounds it. Tiledmedia supports the high-quality live streaming infrastructure, while LiveLike enables community-driven features that turn solitary viewing into a shared social activity. Together, these partner technologies allow broadcasters to surface AI-generated quizzes, polls, and other interactive elements directly within the live stream, creating a cohesive experience where fans participate alongside the broadcast rather than separately from it.

## Enhancing Reach and Quality with Partner Technologies

### Global Localization with SyncWords

To extend the reach of live sports beyond regional audiences, the demo incorporates SyncWords, which uses AI to localize broadcast content in real time. This capability allows broadcasters to match language preferences across diverse markets, breaking down barriers that have historically limited international viewership and enabling a single live event to resonate with a global audience.

### Premium Home Viewing with IMAX

Complementing the localization layer, the demo also leverages IMAX technology to elevate the at-home viewing experience. By delivering IMAX-quality picture and sound for live sports, the platform brings a premium, cinema-grade presentation into the living room, ensuring that fans who engage with quizzes, community features, and localized content also enjoy the highest possible audiovisual fidelity.

## Outcomes and Strategic Value

The demo demonstrates that broadcasters can move beyond static streaming by orchestrating AWS services and partner solutions into a single, intelligent fan engagement platform. The combination of agentic content generation, interactive community tools, AI-powered localization, and premium-quality delivery produces a viewing experience that is simultaneously personalized, participatory, and globally accessible. For broadcasters, the result is a clear pathway to higher engagement, broader audience reach, and deeper fan loyalty in an increasingly competitive media landscape.

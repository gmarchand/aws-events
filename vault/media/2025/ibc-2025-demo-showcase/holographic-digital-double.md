# Holographic Digital Double: Reimagining Audience Engagement with Generative AI

## Overview of the Innovation

Media organizations face mounting pressure to deliver personalized, memorable customer experiences that stand out in an increasingly competitive landscape. To address this challenge, AWS has developed a holographic digital double demonstration that showcases how generative AI can transform traditional audience interactions into dynamic, conversational encounters. This innovation merges advanced speech recognition, large language models, and holographic display technology to bring a lifelike digital presenter into direct dialogue with users.

The demonstration centers on a holographic representation of Ruth Buscombe, a well-known Formula 1 TV presenter and race strategist. Rather than functioning as a passive video playback, the digital double engages attendees in real-time conversations, offering tailored recommendations and information drawn from contextual knowledge about the event environment.

## Technical Architecture and Capabilities

### Combining Amazon Transcribe and Amazon Bedrock

The solution relies on a tightly integrated AWS technology stack. Amazon Transcribe handles the speech-to-text component, capturing spoken questions from attendees and converting them into text that the system can process. Amazon Bedrock then provides the generative AI foundation, enabling the digital double to formulate contextually relevant, natural-sounding responses. By pairing these services, the experience achieves the conversational fluency required to feel authentic, while remaining grounded in accurate, up-to-date information about the surrounding context.

### Delivering a Realistic Interactive Persona

What distinguishes this demo from conventional chatbots or virtual assistants is the holographic presentation layer combined with the persona-driven AI behavior. Attendees do not simply read or hear answers; they converse with a visually rendered digital human modeled on a recognizable industry figure. The system maintains the personality and expertise associated with Ruth Buscombe, allowing the interaction to feel both personal and credible.

## Practical Application at IBC 2025

### Guided Discovery of the AWS Booth and Event

At IBC 2025, the holographic digital double serves as an interactive concierge for attendees. Visitors can ask the digital Ruth Buscombe what to see across the show floor, and the system responds with personalized recommendations. Beyond general event guidance, the digital double also explains the innovations featured throughout the AWS booth, helping attendees navigate complex technical demonstrations and identify the experiences most relevant to their interests.

This use case illustrates a broader outcome that media organizations can replicate: deploying generative AI–powered digital humans to scale personalized engagement, deepen brand storytelling, and create memorable touchpoints that traditional kiosks or static displays cannot match.

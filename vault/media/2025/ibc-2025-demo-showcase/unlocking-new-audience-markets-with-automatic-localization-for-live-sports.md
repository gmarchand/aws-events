# Unlocking New Audience Markets with Automatic Localization for Live Sports

## Expanding Global Reach Through Real-Time Localization

Live sports possess a unique ability to unite audiences across the world, yet capturing the attention of new markets demands far more than simply broadcasting the game. Modern viewers expect experiences that feel personal, immediate, and culturally relevant, and they want commentary and on-screen information presented in their preferred language. To meet these expectations, broadcasters and streaming providers must move beyond traditional delivery models and embrace localization workflows that operate at the speed of live action. AWS Elemental Media Services, combined with generative AI, offers a path to this transformation by automating translation and subtitling processes that previously required extensive manual effort and significant production time.

## Delivering Multilingual Experiences with AWS Elemental and Generative AI

### Automating Translation and Subtitling Workflows

The demonstrated solution shows how broadcasters can integrate AWS Elemental Media Services with generative AI capabilities to produce live commentary and on-screen text in multiple languages with minimal latency. The workflow ingests live sports feeds, applies AI-driven speech recognition to capture commentary, and uses generative models to translate audio and graphics into target languages in near real time. This automation eliminates the operational complexity of staffing multilingual commentary teams for every market, allowing rights holders to scale localized output efficiently and consistently across a wide portfolio of events.

### Enhancing Cultural Resonance and Viewer Engagement

Beyond simple translation, the workflow emphasizes cultural resonance, ensuring that localized output reflects the nuances and expectations of each target audience rather than producing generic or literal translations. By delivering subtitles, captions, and translated commentary in sync with the live broadcast, sports brands can foster deeper connections with fans in regions that were previously underserved. This approach helps broadcasters broaden their global footprint, unlock new revenue opportunities in emerging markets, and build lasting viewer engagement by treating every audience as a primary audience.

## Strategic Outcomes for Broadcasters and Streaming Providers

The combination of AWS Elemental Media Services and generative AI represents a meaningful shift in how live sports content can be distributed at global scale. Broadcasters gain the ability to enter new markets quickly without the cost burden of dedicated localization teams for each language, while streaming providers can differentiate their offerings through richer, more inclusive viewer experiences. Ultimately, automatic localization positions sports brands to capitalize on the worldwide appetite for live competition, transforming language barriers from a limitation into an opportunity for sustained audience growth.

# Avid on AWS: Transforming Media Production in the Cloud

## Breaking Free from Traditional Production Constraints

Media production companies have long operated under significant limitations imposed by fixed facility locations, intricate on-premises infrastructure, and the necessity of physically gathering creative talent in shared workspaces. These constraints have historically restricted how production teams collaborate, scale their operations, and respond to evolving project demands. Avid on AWS fundamentally addresses these challenges by relocating production capabilities to the cloud, enabling global teams to work together seamlessly using the familiar Avid tools they already rely on, including Media Composer and NEXIS storage. This shift allows organizations to deploy fully functional production studios instantly across any geography, liberating artists and editors to contribute from wherever they happen to be.

## Enabling Hybrid Workflows and Centralized Security

A central advantage of the Avid on AWS approach lies in its support for hybrid production environments. Through Avid NEXIS Remote, customers can continue leveraging their existing on-premises workstations while simultaneously scaling their operations with cloud-based virtual workstations whenever additional capacity is required. This flexibility ensures that organizations protect their prior infrastructure investments while gaining the elasticity that cloud computing provides. Throughout these workflows, media assets remain centrally secured within the AWS Cloud, giving production teams confidence that their valuable content is protected even as it becomes more accessible to distributed contributors.

### Accelerating Creativity Through AI and Real-Time Collaboration

Beyond infrastructure transformation, Avid on AWS enhances the creative process itself through advanced tooling. AI-powered capabilities such as Quickture automate the generation of rough cuts, dramatically accelerating the editing workflow and freeing creative professionals to focus on higher-value storytelling decisions. Complementing these automation features, Evercast delivers real-time collaboration with color-accurate 4K streaming, allowing producers and editors to review work together with the visual fidelity required for professional decision-making, even when separated by continents. Together, these capabilities transform geographically dispersed teams into cohesive creative units, redefining what is possible in modern media production.

# Transforming the Viewing Experience Through Extended Reality

## The Shifting Landscape of Media Consumption

The media and television industry is undergoing a profound transformation as audience viewing habits evolve and traditional business models face mounting pressure to adapt. Broadcasters are actively seeking new ways to engage viewers and unlock fresh revenue streams, and extended reality (XR) technology has emerged as a compelling avenue for innovation. By optimizing entertainment and live sports content for the latest wearable headsets and smart glasses, broadcasters can deliver immersive experiences that go far beyond what conventional screens offer, while still allowing viewers to remain aware of their physical surroundings.

## Reimagining Live Sports and Entertainment Through XR

Modern XR experiences elevate viewer engagement by layering rich, contextual information directly into the field of view. Audiences can simultaneously access real-time statistics, switch between multiple camera angles, explore player insights, and view graphic overlays without losing focus on the action itself. This combination of immersion and information turns passive viewing into an interactive, personalized experience, opening the door for broadcasters to differentiate their offerings and capture new audiences who expect richer, more participatory content.

### Accedo and Stats Perform Powering Immersive Sports on AWS

The demo showcases how AWS Partners Accedo and Stats Perform combine their strengths to bring this vision to life. Accedo's Xtend platform delivers immersive content seamlessly across both augmented reality and virtual reality headsets, providing the foundation for cross-device XR experiences. Stats Perform's Opta AI Studio complements this by supplying real-time data, AI-generated graphics, and comprehensive sports statistics that enrich the viewing environment with authoritative, up-to-the-moment insights.

### Hands-Free Interaction with Amazon Nova Sonic

The experience is further transformed by Amazon Nova Sonic, which introduces natural, hands-free voice conversations into the XR environment. Viewers can interact in multiple languages with an AI-powered virtual co-commentator that offers match insights, answers questions, and helps personalize the experience on demand. This conversational layer removes friction from the interface, making the technology feel intuitive and accessible while deepening the sense of connection between the audience and the live event. Together, these capabilities demonstrate how AWS and its partners are enabling broadcasters to build the next generation of viewing experiences.

# Generating Highlights and Trailers with Agents

## Transforming Promotional Content Creation Through AI Agents

Media companies and content marketers face mounting pressure to produce promotional videos quickly and at scale, yet traditional editing workflows remain resource-intensive and time-consuming. This demonstration showcases how agentic AI, powered by Amazon Bedrock, fundamentally reshapes the production of trailers, promos, and highlight reels by allowing teams to generate compelling promotional content from existing footage using only natural language prompts. Rather than spending days assembling clips manually, creative teams can now describe their vision in plain language and let specialized agents handle the heavy lifting of content discovery, selection, and assembly.

## How Specialized Agents Orchestrate the Production Workflow

The solution relies on a coordinated system of specialized agents, each responsible for a distinct phase of the promotional video production pipeline. These agents collaborate across content extraction, scene selection, and final assembly, working seamlessly with diverse content types including news segments, episodic television, and sports footage. By dividing the workflow into focused responsibilities, the system can intelligently identify the most relevant moments within source material, evaluate their narrative or emotional weight, and stitch them together into coherent promotional pieces that align with the user's original prompt.

### Maintaining Creative Control Through Human Validation

Although automation drives the bulk of the workflow, the demonstration emphasizes that human judgment remains central to creative output. Strategic checkpoints are built into the agentic process, allowing editors and producers to validate decisions at key creative junctures, such as scene selection or narrative pacing. This hybrid approach ensures that the final output reflects brand voice and editorial intent while still benefiting from the dramatic acceleration that agents provide.

### Scaling Campaigns and Maximizing Audience Engagement

By converting traditionally manual editing tasks into a scalable, agent-driven operation, media organizations can dramatically accelerate campaign creation and respond more nimbly to audience trends and timely opportunities. The demonstration illustrates how teams can produce more promotional assets across more titles and platforms without expanding headcount, ultimately maximizing audience engagement and the commercial value of their existing content libraries.

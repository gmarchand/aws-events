# AWS at NAB Show 2025: Event Overview

## Showcasing the Future of Media and Entertainment

AWS is bringing its full media and entertainment vision to NAB Show 2025, taking place April 5–9 at the Las Vegas Convention Center. Anchored by the theme *Create. Connect. Captivate.*, the event invites attendees to explore how cloud technologies and generative AI are reshaping content creation, distribution, and audience engagement. Through a combination of hands-on demonstrations, immersive experiences, and thought leadership, AWS aims to help media professionals reimagine what is possible across the entire content lifecycle.

## On-Site Experiences and Activations

### Booth W1701 and the AI Innovation Pavilion

At the center of the AWS presence is booth W1701, where attendees can engage directly with innovative demonstrations spanning production, post-production, broadcast, streaming, and direct-to-consumer workflows. Complementing the booth, the AI Innovation Pavilion highlights the latest advances in generative AI, showcasing how machine learning and foundation models can accelerate creative tasks, enhance content personalization, and unlock new forms of storytelling for media organizations of every size.

### Esport Racing Challenge and Leadership Sessions

Beyond the technical demonstrations, AWS is hosting a live Esport Racing Challenge in the West Hall lobby, offering an interactive activation that blends entertainment with cloud-powered gaming experiences. Rounding out the program are inspiring leadership sessions designed to convene industry executives, creators, and technologists around the trends shaping the future of media. Together, these activities position NAB Show 2025 as a destination where attendees can not only see emerging AWS capabilities in action but also connect with the people and ideas driving the next wave of media innovation.

# AWS at NAB Show 2025: Day 1 Highlights

## Showcasing the Future of Media and Entertainment

AWS opened its presence at NAB Show 2025 in Las Vegas with a bold theme — Create, Connect, Captivate — inviting media and entertainment professionals to explore how cloud and generative AI are reshaping the industry. Held at the Las Vegas Convention Center from April 5 through 9, the event positioned AWS as a central hub for innovation, drawing creators, broadcasters, and technology leaders to experience the latest advancements in content production, distribution, and audience engagement.

## Immersive Experiences Across the Venue

### Interactive Demonstrations and Live Activations

At the heart of the AWS experience was booth W1701, where attendees engaged directly with cutting-edge demonstrations highlighting how AWS services support the full media workflow. Complementing the booth, a live Esport Racing Challenge took over the West Hall lobby, blending entertainment with technology to illustrate the real-time capabilities of AWS infrastructure. The AI Innovation Pavilion served as a dedicated space to showcase generative AI applications transforming creative workflows, from content generation to personalization, giving visitors a hands-on look at tools poised to redefine production pipelines.

### Thought Leadership and Industry Dialogue

Beyond the demonstrations, AWS hosted a series of leadership sessions designed to inspire and inform. These conversations brought together industry experts to discuss strategic shifts in media and entertainment, the practical adoption of AI, and the future of audience experiences. The sessions reinforced AWS's role not only as a technology provider but as a partner guiding the industry through its ongoing transformation.

## Setting the Tone for the Week Ahead

Day 1 established a strong foundation for the remainder of NAB Show 2025, combining technical depth with experiential engagement. By weaving together generative AI showcases, interactive challenges, and executive-level discussions, AWS demonstrated a clear commitment to helping media and entertainment companies create more efficiently, connect more broadly, and captivate audiences in new ways throughout the week.

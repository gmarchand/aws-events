# Elevating Streaming Quality with AI: Prime Video's Innovative Approach

## Introduction

At AWS re:Invent 2025, Prime Video presented its strategy for harnessing artificial intelligence to transform the global streaming experience for millions of concurrent viewers. The session focused on how generative AI and AWS services such as Amazon Bedrock and Amazon Q underpin a quality-first approach to delivering premium live and on-demand content. Prime Video framed AI not as an experimental add-on but as core infrastructure for ensuring that every stream — from blockbuster films to live sporting events — meets exceptionally high standards of visual quality, accuracy, and viewer trust.

## Applying Generative AI to Premium Live Sports

### Enhancing the Viewing Experience for NBA and NFL Broadcasts

A central theme of the talk was Prime Video's use of generative AI to elevate the quality of live sports content, particularly NBA and NFL games. Live sports present unique technical challenges: variable network conditions, fast-moving action, demanding encoding requirements, and viewers who expect broadcast-grade clarity at scale. Prime Video described how AI-driven systems improve picture quality, optimize encoding decisions, and adapt streams in real time to maintain a premium experience even under fluctuating conditions. By leveraging generative models, the team can intelligently enhance frames, reduce artifacts, and ensure that high-stakes live moments reach audiences worldwide without degradation.

### Scaling Quality Across Millions of Concurrent Streams

The engineering challenge extends beyond a single broadcast. Prime Video must sustain consistent quality of experience for millions of simultaneous viewers across diverse devices, regions, and bandwidth profiles. The session highlighted how AWS's elastic infrastructure, combined with AI-driven monitoring, allows the platform to detect quality issues proactively and intervene before they affect the audience. This combination of generative AI inference and cloud-native scalability is what makes broadcast-grade live streaming feasible on a global scale.

## AI for Artwork and Content Moderation

### Automating Artwork Quality with Amazon Bedrock

Prime Video also detailed an AI system dedicated to artwork generation, validation, and moderation. Title artwork is a critical driver of viewer engagement, but ensuring that thousands of images meet brand, localization, and compliance standards is a substantial operational burden. Using foundation models accessed through Amazon Bedrock, Prime Video automates the assessment of artwork for visual quality, text accuracy, regional appropriateness, and adherence to creative guidelines. This dramatically reduces manual review effort while improving consistency across the global catalog.

### Quality Moderation with Amazon Q

Complementing the artwork pipeline, Prime Video uses Amazon Q to support quality moderation workflows. The system helps identify defects, flag anomalies, and surface insights to human reviewers, allowing operations teams to focus on the most impactful issues. By integrating generative AI assistants directly into editorial and quality-assurance processes, Prime Video shortens turnaround times and strengthens the reliability of its content pipeline.

## Outcomes and Key Takeaways

The session concluded by emphasizing how AI has become essential to maintaining Prime Video's quality bar at global scale. Generative AI enhances live sports streams in real time, automates artwork validation, and powers intelligent moderation, all running on AWS services that deliver the necessary scalability and resilience. The overarching message to attendees was that quality of experience in modern streaming depends on tightly integrating AI throughout the content lifecycle — from ingestion and encoding to artwork and viewer-facing delivery — and that AWS's portfolio, particularly Bedrock and Amazon Q, provides the building blocks to make this approach operational rather than aspirational.

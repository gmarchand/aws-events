# Powering the Gundam Metaverse with Amazon GameLift Streams

## Building a Globally Scalable Cloud Streaming Platform

Bandai Namco Entertainment partnered with AWS to bring its Gundam metaverse to a worldwide audience by transforming it into a cloud-native streaming experience built on Amazon GameLift Streams. The session details how the company moved beyond traditional client installations to deliver high-fidelity metaverse content directly through web browsers, eliminating download barriers and enabling instant access for players in more than twenty countries. By leveraging GameLift Streams as the foundation, Bandai Namco achieved the rendering quality required for an immersive Gundam experience while maintaining the accessibility expected of modern web platforms.

The architectural strategy centered on a multi-runtime design that supports both performance-intensive metaverse interactions and lightweight, instant browser sessions. This dual approach allows the platform to serve casual visitors who want immediate entry alongside dedicated users seeking deeper engagement, all without compromising visual quality or responsiveness. Network performance visualization tools were integrated into the operational stack so engineers could monitor latency, throughput, and session health across the global footprint in real time.

## Optimizing Performance, Cost, and Capacity Across Regions

A central theme of the presentation is the balance between minimizing session startup times and controlling infrastructure costs at global scale. Bandai Namco deployed across six AWS regions and implemented dynamic capacity management that adjusts running fleet size based on real-time demand signals. This elasticity ensures users in any geography receive low-latency sessions while idle capacity is trimmed to avoid unnecessary spend. The speakers walk through the scaling heuristics, regional traffic patterns, and the engineering decisions that allowed the team to keep cold-start delays minimal even during traffic surges tied to in-game events and promotions.

### Bridging Virtual and Physical Commerce Through Amazon Integration

Beyond gameplay, the session highlights how the Gundam metaverse incorporates Amazon integrations to create immersive shopping experiences inside the virtual world. Users can browse and purchase merchandise without leaving the metaverse, blurring the line between entertainment and commerce. This integration demonstrates a practical pathway for entertainment IP holders to monetize virtual environments while delivering added value to fans, and it serves as a blueprint for other brands considering similar virtual-to-physical commerce bridges.

### Key Takeaways for Builders

Attendees leave with concrete guidance on dynamic scaling strategies, multi-region deployment patterns, and runtime architectures suited to streaming-based metaverse platforms. The Bandai Namco case study illustrates that cloud streaming, when paired with intelligent capacity orchestration and thoughtful integration of commerce features, can deliver console-quality experiences globally through a browser while remaining cost-effective and operationally sustainable.

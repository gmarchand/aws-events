# Automating Business Processes with Amazon Quick Suite

## Session Overview

At AWS re:Invent 2025, session BIZ224 introduced how organizations can automate virtually any business process using Amazon Quick Suite, with a particular focus on two complementary capabilities: Amazon Quick Flows and Amazon Quick Automate. The session positioned these tools as a way to democratize agentic AI across the enterprise, enabling employees at every level—not just developers or data scientists—to safely build automations that accelerate their daily work. The central premise was that agentic AI has matured to the point where business users can confidently delegate multi-step tasks to AI-powered workflows, and Quick Suite provides the guardrails, integrations, and ease of use required to make this practical at enterprise scale.

## Quick Flows and Quick Automate in Action

### Capabilities and Use Cases

Quick Flows targets lighter-weight, user-driven automations where individuals string together AI actions to handle repetitive knowledge work, while Quick Automate addresses more complex, multi-system business processes that traditionally required custom development or rigid RPA tooling. Together, they enable agents to read and write across enterprise applications, reason about unstructured data, and execute end-to-end workflows with human oversight where needed. The session highlighted real-world deployments at 3M, Kitsa, dLocal, and Propulse Labs, each of which used Quick Suite to compress cycle times, reduce manual effort, and improve customer outcomes by removing operational friction from processes that had previously consumed significant employee time.

### Customer Spotlight: Jabil

A central portion of the session featured Jabil, who shared their automation journey using Quick Suite. Jabil described how they identified high-value processes ripe for agentic automation, the implementation approach they took to build trust with business stakeholders, and the measurable gains they achieved in productivity and accuracy. They also outlined their forward-looking vision for scaling automation enterprise-wide, emphasizing the importance of governance, reusable patterns, and empowering business teams to own their automations rather than routing every request through central IT.

## Key Takeaways for Implementation

The session closed with practical guidance for organizations beginning their own agentic automation journeys. Attendees were encouraged to start with well-scoped, high-friction processes where the return on investment is clear, to lean on Quick Suite's built-in safety and governance features to maintain control as adoption grows, and to invest in enabling business users so that automation becomes a shared organizational capability rather than a bottleneck. The overarching message was that agentic automation, when paired with the right platform, can fundamentally change how teams work—shifting human attention away from rote execution and toward higher-value judgment, creativity, and customer engagement.

# Contextual Ads and New Ad Formats for Live Sports

## Overview and Industry Challenge

Media publishers and broadcasters operate in an environment where maximizing advertising revenue must be carefully balanced against the risk of alienating viewers with intrusive or repetitive ad experiences. Traditional ad formats, particularly during high-value live sporting events, often interrupt the flow of content and fail to capitalize on the heightened engagement that live audiences bring. This demonstration addresses that tension by presenting a new approach to ad delivery designed for content creators, streaming platforms, and advertisers seeking to elevate viewer experience while opening additional revenue streams.

## A Joint Solution Built on AWS Elemental MediaTailor and InTheGame

The solution showcased is built on an integration between AWS Elemental MediaTailor and AWS Partner InTheGame. MediaTailor provides the server-side ad insertion and personalization backbone, enabling broadcasters to stitch targeted advertising seamlessly into live and on-demand streams at scale. InTheGame extends this foundation with interactive and shoppable ad formats specifically tailored for large-scale live sporting events, where viewer attention and emotional investment are at their peak.

### Generative AI and E-commerce Integration

A central element of the demonstration is the use of generative AI to produce contextual, personalized ad creative that aligns with the moments unfolding in the live broadcast. Rather than relying on static, pre-produced spots, the system can generate ads that reflect the context of the game, the preferences of the individual viewer, and the products most likely to resonate. Combined with embedded e-commerce integrations, these ads become directly shoppable, allowing viewers to engage with and purchase products without leaving the viewing experience.

### Outcomes for Publishers and Advertisers

The combined approach unlocks new monetization models for media companies while delivering measurable improvements in engagement and advertiser performance. By replacing disruptive interruptions with personalized, interactive experiences, publishers can sustain audience attention throughout live events, advertisers gain higher-quality interactions and conversion opportunities, and viewers receive ads that feel relevant rather than intrusive. The demonstration ultimately illustrates how generative AI, server-side ad insertion, and partner-driven interactive formats can together redefine advertising strategy for live sports streaming.

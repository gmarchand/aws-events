# AWS at IBC Show 2025: Friday Recap

## Setting the Stage in Amsterdam

The opening day of IBC 2025 in Amsterdam delivered a strong showing for AWS, as the company welcomed media and entertainment professionals to its booth in Hall 5, location 5.C90. Running from September 12 through 15, the event positioned AWS at the center of conversations around content creation, workflow connectivity, and audience engagement, reinforcing the company's commitment to the broadcast and media industry through the AWS for M&E initiative.

## Showcasing Innovation and Customer Stories

### Demonstrations and the Future Tech Stage

The AWS booth served as a hub of activity throughout Friday, featuring live demonstrations that illustrated how cloud technology is reshaping media workflows. Complementing these hands-on experiences, the Future Tech stage hosted speaker sessions with prominent customers, including Formula 1, who shared real-world examples of how AWS services power their operations. These sessions highlighted practical applications of AWS solutions in high-performance, content-intensive environments.

### Networking and Industry Engagement

Beyond the technical content, the day captured the energy of the broader IBC community through networking events that brought together customers, partners, and industry leaders. The atmosphere at the booth reflected both the excitement of returning to one of the industry's premier gatherings and the momentum behind AWS's role in enabling the next generation of media experiences.

## Continuing the Experience

For attendees who missed the live demonstrations, AWS made its IBC 2025 content accessible online, ensuring that the insights and innovations showcased in Amsterdam reach a wider audience. The Friday recap set the tone for the remaining days of the conference, signaling continued opportunities to explore how AWS is helping media organizations create content, connect workflows, and captivate audiences worldwide.

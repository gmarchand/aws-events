# AWS Elemental Turns 10: A Decade of Media Innovation

## Reflecting on a Transformative Acquisition

At IBC Show 2025 in Amsterdam, AWS celebrated a significant milestone: the tenth anniversary of the AWS Elemental acquisition. Original team members who have been part of the journey since the acquisition announcement a decade ago gathered to reflect on how far the organization has come. Their conversations highlighted the pivotal role Elemental has played in shaping the modern media and entertainment landscape, particularly in how live and on-demand video content is processed, packaged, and delivered at scale through cloud infrastructure.

## A Journey of Growth and Industry Impact

### From Acquisition to Cloud Leadership

The retrospective shared by founding team members traced Elemental's evolution from a specialized video processing company into a foundational pillar of AWS's media services portfolio. Over the past ten years, the team has worked to bring broadcast-grade video capabilities to the cloud, enabling content providers worldwide to scale their operations, reduce infrastructure complexity, and reach global audiences with high-quality streaming experiences. The team's reflections emphasized how the acquisition accelerated innovation, allowing Elemental's technology to integrate deeply with the broader AWS ecosystem and serve customers across broadcasting, streaming, and live events.

### Looking Ahead to the Future of Media

Beyond celebrating past achievements, team members expressed enthusiasm for what lies ahead in media innovation. The conversation pointed toward continued investment in advanced video technologies, evolving customer needs around personalized and immersive viewing experiences, and the integration of emerging capabilities such as AI-driven workflows. The tenth anniversary serves not only as a moment of reflection but also as a launchpad for the next decade of advancements in cloud-based media services, with the team committed to driving the industry forward alongside its customers and partners.

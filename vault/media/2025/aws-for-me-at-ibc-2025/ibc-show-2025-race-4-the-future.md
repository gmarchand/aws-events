# Race 4 the Future at IBC 2025

## Event Overview

The inaugural Race 4 the Future charity run took place on September 13, 2025, during the IBC Show in Amsterdam, bringing together media and entertainment industry professionals for a meaningful cause. Held alongside the broader IBC 2025 conference running from September 12 to 15, this event marked a new chapter in a long-standing tradition of charitable engagement within the broadcast technology community. The run drew participants from across the industry who came together not only to celebrate fitness and camaraderie but also to support a cause aimed at transforming the future of media technology.

## Legacy and Inspiration

### Honoring the AWS Elemental 4K 4Charity Tradition

Race 4 the Future builds directly on the legacy of the AWS Elemental 4K 4Charity Fun Run, a beloved industry tradition that has long united broadcast professionals around philanthropic goals. By carrying forward this spirit, the new event preserves the values established by its predecessor while opening fresh opportunities to engage the community in support of emerging talent.

### The Sam Blackman Medal

A centerpiece of the event is the Sam Blackman Medal, awarded in honor of the visionary leader whose influence continues to shape the industry. This recognition serves as a tribute to Blackman's contributions and ensures that his legacy of innovation and generosity inspires future generations of media technology professionals.

## Supporting Rise Academy and Future Talent

### Opening Doors for Underrepresented Youth

Proceeds from the run benefit Rise Academy, an organization dedicated to opening doors for underrepresented young people in media technology. By directing industry support toward Rise Academy's mission, Race 4 the Future actively contributes to building a more diverse and inclusive talent pipeline for the broadcast and media sectors.

### Community Spirit and Celebration

Beyond the serious purpose, the event embraced a festive atmosphere with team competitions and fancy dress awards that encouraged creativity and camaraderie among participants. This blend of charitable purpose, friendly competition, and celebration of both legacy and emerging talent captured the essence of what Race 4 the Future aspires to be: a meaningful gathering that honors the past while investing in the future of the industry.

# AWS Elemental 10th Anniversary Celebration at IBC 2025

## Marking a Decade of Media Innovation

AWS hosted an exclusive customer appreciation event at the Heineken Experience in Amsterdam during IBC 2025, commemorating ten years of AWS Elemental Media Services. Held from September 12–15, 2025, the celebration brought together industry leaders, customers, and partners to recognize a decade of transformation in cloud-based media technology and to look ahead at what comes next for the industry.

## An Evening of Connection and Celebration

The anniversary gathering created an atmosphere designed to foster meaningful connections among attendees. Guests enjoyed drinks, gourmet food, and live music throughout the venue, while interactive experiences such as Formula 1 simulators added energy and entertainment to the evening. The setting at the iconic Heineken Experience provided a memorable backdrop that complemented the milestone nature of the occasion, encouraging both relaxed networking and lively conversation among media and broadcast professionals.

### Reflecting on Ten Years and Looking Forward

Beyond the festivities, the event served as a moment to toast the achievements of AWS Elemental over the past decade and to celebrate the customers and partners who have shaped its journey. Conversations throughout the night centered on the evolution of media services and the future of streaming, broadcast, and content delivery technologies. The celebration reinforced AWS Elemental's ongoing commitment to its customer community and signaled continued investment in advancing the media technology landscape in the years ahead.

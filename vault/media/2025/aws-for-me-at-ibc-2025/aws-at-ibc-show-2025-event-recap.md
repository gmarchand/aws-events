# AWS at IBC Show 2025: Event Recap

## Overview of the Event

AWS participated in the IBC Show 2025, held September 12–15 in Amsterdam, Netherlands, delivering a four-day showcase of innovation tailored to the media and entertainment industry. The recap highlights an energetic gathering that brought together AWS teams, partners, and customers to explore the latest advancements in cloud-based media technology. The event emphasized AWS's three strategic pillars for the industry: helping organizations create content, connect workflows, and captivate audiences.

## Highlights from the AWS Presence

### Showcasing Emerging Technologies

The AWS booth served as a focal point for demonstrating cutting-edge solutions, with agentic AI taking center stage as one of the standout technologies presented to attendees. These live demonstrations illustrated how AWS is advancing the application of artificial intelligence in media workflows, giving visitors a tangible sense of how next-generation tools can transform content creation and distribution.

### Customer Stories and Industry Collaboration

Beyond product demonstrations, AWS used the AWS and NVIDIA Innovation Stage to share 25 customer use cases over the course of the week. These sessions provided real-world context for the technologies on display, demonstrating how broadcasters, studios, and media companies are already leveraging AWS to solve pressing industry challenges. The collaboration with NVIDIA underscored the importance of strategic partnerships in delivering high-performance, AI-driven media solutions.

### Connecting with the Community

A central theme of the recap was the value of in-person connection. Throughout the four days, AWS engaged with customers and industry professionals, fostering conversations that reinforced relationships and surfaced new opportunities for collaboration. The atmosphere captured in the recap reflected both the technical depth of the program and the community spirit that defined AWS's presence at IBC 2025.

## Key Takeaways

The IBC Show 2025 reinforced AWS's commitment to the media and entertainment sector through a combination of technology demonstrations, customer storytelling, and meaningful engagement. By spotlighting agentic AI, partnering with NVIDIA, and amplifying customer success stories, AWS positioned itself as a driving force behind the future of media workflows. Attendees left with a clear view of how AWS continues to enable the industry to create, connect, and captivate at scale.

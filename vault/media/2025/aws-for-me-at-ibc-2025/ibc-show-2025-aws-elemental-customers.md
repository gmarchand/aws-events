# AWS Elemental at IBC Show 2025: Celebrating a Decade of Customer Partnerships

## A Decade of Media Innovation

At the IBC 2025 booth in Amsterdam, the AWS Elemental team reflected on ten years of collaboration with customers who have fundamentally shaped the trajectory of cloud-based media technology. The team emphasized that the journey since AWS acquired Elemental has been defined less by the technology itself and more by the pioneering broadcasters and streaming platforms that have pushed the boundaries of what is possible. These partnerships have driven AWS Elemental to continuously evolve its services, ensuring they meet the demands of an industry undergoing rapid transformation.

## Customer-Driven Transformation

### Pioneering Broadcasters and Streaming Platforms

The AWS Elemental team highlighted how working alongside leading broadcasters and innovative streaming services has enabled the development of breakthrough solutions for video processing, delivery, and monetization. By engaging directly with customers facing real-world challenges—such as scaling live events, transitioning legacy workflows to the cloud, and delivering high-quality experiences across diverse devices—AWS has been able to refine and expand its media services portfolio. This collaborative approach has positioned customers not merely as users of technology but as co-creators in shaping the future of broadcast and streaming.

### Building Breakthrough Solutions Together

The interview underscored that many of the advancements in AWS Elemental's offerings originated from customer needs and feedback. Whether enabling traditional broadcasters to embrace cloud-native architectures or empowering new streaming entrants to launch globally, the team's work has consistently centered on solving practical problems through close partnership. This customer-first methodology has been a cornerstone of AWS Elemental's success over the past decade.

## Looking Ahead to the Next Chapter

### Vision for the Future of Media

Looking forward, the AWS Elemental team shared their vision for continued innovation in the media industry, signaling that the first decade is only the beginning. They expressed enthusiasm about emerging opportunities to further transform how content is created, delivered, and experienced, and reaffirmed AWS's commitment to deepening customer partnerships. The message from IBC 2025 was clear: AWS Elemental intends to remain a driving force in media technology, working hand-in-hand with customers to define what comes next in broadcasting and streaming.

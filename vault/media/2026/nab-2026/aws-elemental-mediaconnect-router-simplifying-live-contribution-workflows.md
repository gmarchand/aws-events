# AWS Elemental MediaConnect Router: Simplifying Live Contribution Workflows

## Modernizing Broadcast Contribution in the Cloud

Broadcasters have long relied on complex on-premises infrastructure to manage live contribution workflows, where multiple live feeds from globally distributed sources must be routed, switched, and monitored in real time. As the industry modernizes, the cloud presents a compelling opportunity to streamline these operations while preserving the reliability and immediacy that live broadcasting demands. AWS Elemental MediaConnect Router addresses this challenge directly, offering centralized management of live and linear feeds from contributors located anywhere in the world. By bringing traditional switching capabilities into the cloud, broadcasters can reduce the complexity of physical infrastructure while gaining the flexibility to operate at a global scale.

## Demonstrating Centralized Routing and Switching

### Multiple Control Interfaces for Operational Flexibility

The demonstration showcases how MediaConnect Router enables real-time feed routing and switching through several complementary interfaces. A physical remote control panel (RCP) provides a familiar tactile experience for operators accustomed to traditional broadcast environments, ensuring that the transition to cloud-based workflows does not disrupt established practices. Alongside the RCP, a custom-built operator interface illustrates how broadcasters can tailor control surfaces to their specific operational needs, while the native AWS Elemental MediaConnect Router console offers direct access to the service's full capabilities. Together, these interfaces demonstrate that cloud-based routing can match, and in many ways exceed, the responsiveness and usability of legacy hardware-based systems.

### Integrated Monitoring Through Partner Solutions

To complement routing and switching, the demonstration incorporates an AWS Partner Solution from TAG Video Systems, which provides continuous monitoring of feed health and automated alerting. This integration ensures that operators maintain full visibility into the status of every contribution feed, allowing issues to be identified and addressed before they impact viewers. The combination of native AWS routing capabilities with specialized third-party monitoring illustrates how the AWS ecosystem supports complete, production-ready broadcast workflows rather than isolated components.

## Outcomes for Broadcasters

The result of this approach is a contribution workflow that delivers traditional broadcast switching capabilities in a cloud-native form. Broadcasters reduce their dependence on costly and rigid on-premises infrastructure, gain the operational flexibility to manage feeds across regions and continents from a single control point, and benefit from a workflow that scales with their needs. By unifying routing, switching, and monitoring through MediaConnect Router and partner integrations, organizations can modernize confidently while preserving the operational rigor that live contribution requires.

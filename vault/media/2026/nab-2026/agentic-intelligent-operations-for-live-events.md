# Agentic Intelligent Operations for Live Events

## The Operational Challenge of Live Broadcasting

Live events such as sports broadcasts, award shows, and breaking news coverage place extraordinary demands on technical infrastructure. When millions of viewers tune in simultaneously, even minor disruptions in the broadcast or streaming chain can compromise the viewer experience and undermine subscriber loyalty. Broadcast and streaming engineers face the difficult task of identifying and resolving issues across highly complex workflows under intense time pressure, often with only seconds to act before audience impact becomes severe. The complexity is amplified by the fragmented nature of modern media delivery, where signals traverse contribution, processing, packaging, and distribution stages, each with its own observability data and potential failure points.

## Applying Agentic AI Across the Signal Chain

This demonstration illustrates how agentic artificial intelligence can transform live event operations by providing end-to-end visibility and automated root cause analysis across the full broadcast and over-the-top (OTT) signal chain. Built on Amazon Bedrock AgentCore and the AWS Strands SDK, the solution deploys four specialized AI agents that work in concert to diagnose technical issues from contribution through distribution. By coordinating intelligent agents rather than relying on isolated monitoring tools, engineering teams gain a unified troubleshooting capability that reasons across domains and surfaces actionable insights faster than traditional approaches allow.

### Integrated Observability and Partner Data

The agents draw on a rich set of telemetry sources to produce comprehensive situational awareness. Observability data from AWS Media Services provides visibility into encoding, packaging, and origin operations, while Common Media Client Data (CMCD) from video players adds critical client-side perspective on playback quality. Streaming analytics from AWS Partners Mux and Hydrolix extend this picture with deep performance and log analytics, enabling the agents to correlate symptoms experienced by viewers with upstream causes in the workflow. This integration delivers full-chain observability that is essential for diagnosing issues that span multiple systems and vendors.

### Outcomes for Engineering Teams

By applying agentic intelligence to live event operations, teams achieve faster root cause identification and meaningfully reduced incident response times. The approach allows engineers to maintain optimal performance throughout high-stakes broadcasts, preserving the quality of experience that keeps subscribers engaged at the moments that matter most. The demonstration ultimately positions agentic AI as a practical accelerator for broadcast and streaming operations, enabling organizations to manage rising workflow complexity without proportionally increasing operational burden.

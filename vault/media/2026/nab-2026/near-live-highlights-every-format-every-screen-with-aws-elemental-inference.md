# Near-Live Highlights Across Every Format and Screen with AWS Elemental Inference

## The Challenge of Multi-Platform Live Content

Modern audiences consume video across an expanding array of platforms that broadcasters do not control, including TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts. Each of these platforms demands content tailored to its specific format, aspect ratio, and viewing style, which creates a significant operational challenge for live broadcasters. Traditional manual reformatting workflows are far too slow to keep pace with audience expectations, while post-production AI tools introduce additional complexity and delay that undermine the immediacy that makes live content compelling in the first place.

This tension between speed, format diversity, and quality has historically forced broadcasters to compromise on at least one dimension. The demonstration featured in this video addresses that gap by showing how AWS Elemental Inference, working in concert with AWS Elemental MediaLive, can deliver publish-ready content across multiple formats within seconds of the original live action.

## A Two-Stage Workflow for Near-Live Publishing

### Intelligent Reframing in Parallel with Encoding

The first stage of the workflow transforms horizontal live action into vertical, publish-ready video within seconds. AWS Elemental Inference applies machine learning to detect and track relevant subjects within the frame, then intelligently reframes the footage for vertical platforms. Crucially, this processing runs in parallel with encoding rather than as a downstream step, which eliminates the latency typically associated with reformatting pipelines. The result is that vertical clips suitable for short-form social platforms become available almost immediately after the live moment occurs, preserving the urgency that drives engagement on those channels.

### AI-Assisted Enrichment Through Natural Language

The second stage introduces an AI agent embedded within a broadcast-grade editing interface and controlled through natural language commands. Rather than navigating complex menus or scripting custom workflows, operators can describe the enrichment they want and the agent executes it. This includes layering performance insights, contextual data overlays, and visual effects onto the reframed content. By combining conversational control with professional editing tooling, the workflow allows broadcasters to add meaningful context and polish without slowing down publication.

## Outcomes and Partner Integration

Together, these two stages convert a single live broadcast into multi-format, data-driven content ready for distribution across owned channels and third-party social platforms. The integration extends further through AWS Partner solutions, which complement the core Elemental services to round out production capabilities. The overall outcome is a pipeline that deepens fan engagement by meeting audiences on their preferred platforms with appropriately formatted, contextually enriched content, all delivered without the delays that traditionally separate live moments from social-ready highlights.

# AI-Assisted Promo Assembly with Creative Control

## Addressing the Scale Challenge in Promotional Content

Media companies and rights holders face a persistent challenge when producing promotional assets such as highlights, trailers, and reels. The need to deliver content across multiple platforms, formats, and languages strains traditional production workflows, which depend heavily on manual scene selection, format adaptation, and localization coordination. As demand grows, scaling these processes without sacrificing editorial standards becomes increasingly difficult, leaving creative teams overwhelmed by repetitive production tasks rather than focused on storytelling and direction.

This demonstration introduces a content composition platform designed to address that imbalance. Rather than replacing human creativity, the platform amplifies it by automating the mechanical aspects of promo assembly while preserving editorial judgment at every critical stage of production.

## Architecture and Partner Ecosystem

### Foundation Models and AI Orchestration

The platform is powered by Amazon Bedrock with Amazon Nova foundation models, complemented by Amazon Elemental Inference for media-specific processing. AI agents interpret natural language prompts to orchestrate the entire promo assembly workflow, from identifying relevant scenes within source footage to adapting outputs for different distribution channels. This agent-driven approach allows creative teams to express their intent conversationally rather than configuring complex technical parameters, dramatically reducing the friction between concept and execution.

### Specialized AWS Partner Capabilities

The solution integrates capabilities from three AWS Partners, each contributing specialized functionality. MomentsLab provides intelligent media indexing and metadata enrichment, enabling precise scene discovery within large content libraries. TwelveLabs contributes advanced video understanding, allowing the system to comprehend context, action, and narrative within footage. Deepdub delivers high-quality localized narration, making multi-language adaptation a seamless part of the workflow rather than a separate downstream process. Together, these partners enable context-aware reframing, intelligent scene selection, and authentic localization at scale.

### Human Decision Points

A defining principle of the platform is structured human oversight at three deliberate checkpoints. Editors first shape the workstream configuration, defining the creative parameters and objectives. They then review the AI-selected scenes to ensure narrative coherence and brand alignment. Finally, they approve the final output before distribution. This three-stage gating ensures that automation accelerates production without bypassing editorial control.

## Outcomes for Creative Teams

The result is a meaningful shift in how promotional content is produced. Campaigns can be created faster, with multi-platform and multi-language delivery scaling without proportional increases in manual effort. More importantly, creative teams reclaim their focus on direction, narrative, and quality rather than spending their time on repetitive assembly work. By combining foundation models, partner technologies, and disciplined human checkpoints, the platform demonstrates a practical model for AI-assisted production that respects creative authority while unlocking new levels of operational efficiency.

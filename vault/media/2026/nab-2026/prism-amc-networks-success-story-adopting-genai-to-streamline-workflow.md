# PRISM: Transforming AMC Networks' Content Workflows with Generative AI

## Overview of the PRISM Platform

AMC Networks has introduced PRISM, an Intelligent Content Search and Discovery Platform that marks a significant step forward in modernizing how the company approaches production and marketing workflows. The platform addresses long-standing inefficiencies in content discovery and asset management by embedding generative AI tooling directly into the creative pipeline. Rather than relying on manual processes to locate, tag, and retrieve media assets, AMC Networks now leverages PRISM to surface relevant content quickly and securely, enabling production and marketing teams to focus on higher-value creative work.

The platform is designed to handle the demands of professional broadcast media, processing full HD and 4K video content while supporting multi-track audio. By unifying these technical capabilities with AI-driven intelligence, PRISM provides a single, performant environment in which AMC Networks can manage its expansive content library at scale.

## Technical Foundation and AI Capabilities

### AWS and TwelveLabs Integration

PRISM is built on the AWS Media Lake platform, which provides the scalable storage and processing backbone needed to handle large volumes of high-resolution video. At the heart of its intelligence layer is the Marengo Video Analysis model from TwelveLabs, accessed through Amazon Bedrock. This integration allows AMC Networks to apply state-of-the-art video understanding directly to its assets without managing the underlying model infrastructure, accelerating time to value while maintaining flexibility to adopt future model improvements.

### Automated Metadata, Intelligent Search, and Secure Access

The platform automates metadata generation across the content library, eliminating the labor-intensive tagging work that previously slowed down production cycles. Building on this enriched metadata, PRISM delivers intelligent search capabilities that allow users to locate specific scenes, moments, or assets through natural, content-aware queries. Security is treated as a first-class requirement, with controls in place to ensure that media assets are accessed only by authorized users. The platform is engineered to meet defined service-level commitments around performance, metadata accuracy, and system availability, giving AMC Networks confidence that PRISM can be relied upon as core production infrastructure.

## Business Impact and Outcomes

By replacing manual content discovery with AI-powered search and automated metadata enrichment, PRISM has reshaped how AMC Networks' production and marketing teams operate. Workflows that once required significant time to navigate large media libraries are now streamlined, freeing creative teams to move faster from concept to delivery. The combination of secure access, reliable performance, and intelligent discovery positions PRISM as a strategic asset that not only improves day-to-day efficiency but also demonstrates how generative AI, when paired with proven cloud and media services, can deliver tangible operational gains for a major media company.

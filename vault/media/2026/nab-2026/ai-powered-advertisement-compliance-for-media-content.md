# AI-Powered Advertisement Compliance for Media Content

## Overview

Media organizations today operate under intense pressure to validate the advertisements they broadcast across an increasingly diverse range of campaign types. Political advertisements demand rigorous funding disclosures and claim verification, pharmaceutical commercials require precise disclaimers, automotive ads must substantiate safety assertions, and consumer product spots need to meet broadcast standards. Manually reviewing this volume and variety of content creates significant bottlenecks, slows approval workflows, and introduces inconsistency in how regulatory and editorial standards are applied. This demonstration presents an AI-powered approach that addresses these challenges by automating the analysis and verification of commercial advertisements through video understanding and agentic AI.

## Solution Architecture and Approach

### Agentic AI Powered by Amazon Bedrock

The solution leverages Amazon Bedrock together with Amazon Bedrock AgentCore to orchestrate a coordinated swarm of AI agents that collaborate on advertisement review. Rather than relying on a single model to perform every task, the architecture distributes responsibilities across specialized agents that work in parallel to evaluate different facets of an advertisement. These agents perform accelerated automated claim validation, conduct fact-checking against authoritative sources, and verify compliance with both regional regulations and internal organizational policies. By combining video understanding capabilities with agent-based reasoning, the system can interpret visual elements, spoken content, and on-screen text in advertisements and assess them against the rules that govern each advertising category.

### Workflow Automation and Compliance Verification

The agents systematically check each advertisement for the disclosures, disclaimers, and substantiations required by its category—whether that involves confirming political funding statements, validating pharmaceutical safety language, or ensuring automotive claims align with documented evidence. This automated workflow replaces large portions of manual review, dramatically reducing the time required to approve or flag an advertisement while maintaining a consistent standard across all submissions.

## Business Outcomes

By adopting this AI-driven compliance solution, media organizations can manage high volumes of advertising content efficiently without sacrificing editorial integrity or regulatory adherence. The technology shortens approval cycles, eliminates review bottlenecks, and enforces uniform compliance across multiple advertising categories. The result is a scalable, reliable process that supports broadcasters in meeting their obligations to regulators, advertisers, and audiences alike, while freeing human reviewers to focus on the most nuanced and judgment-intensive cases.

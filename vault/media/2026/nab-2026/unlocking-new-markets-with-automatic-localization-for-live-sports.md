# Unlocking New Markets with Automatic Localization for Live Sports

## Expanding Global Reach Through Real-Time Localization

Live sports possess a unique power to unite audiences across the world, yet reaching new markets demands far more than simply transmitting a game across borders. Modern fans expect experiences that feel native to their region, delivered in real time and shaped by cultural context as well as language. This demonstration addresses that challenge by showcasing how broadcasters can automatically localize live sports content using AWS, eliminating the high cost and operational complexity traditionally associated with manual localization teams while opening pathways into previously inaccessible markets.

## A Cloud-Based Workflow for Live Multilingual Subtitling

The solution combines automatic speech-to-text conversion with an agentic AI translation workflow powered by Amazon Bedrock and AWS Elemental Media Services. As live commentary streams in, the system transcribes the audio and feeds it into an intelligent translation pipeline that generates subtitles in multiple target languages simultaneously. The agentic approach allows the workflow to handle nuanced decisions during translation, ensuring that sports-specific terminology is preserved accurately and that cultural references remain appropriate for each target audience. Critically, the entire pipeline operates with low latency, allowing translated subtitles to appear within seconds of the original commentary—a requirement non-negotiable for live sports.

### Demonstrated Outcomes and Business Impact

During the live demonstration, viewers see a sports stream being transcribed and translated on the fly, with multiple language subtitles rendered concurrently as the action unfolds. This proves that broadcasters no longer need to choose between speed, accuracy, and scale. By automating localization end-to-end on AWS, rights holders and broadcasters can extend their content into new geographies quickly and economically, transforming localization from a cost center into a growth enabler that broadens fan engagement and unlocks new revenue opportunities worldwide.

# Agents for Advertising: Transforming Campaign Workflows with Agentic AI

## The Challenge Facing Modern Advertising Teams

Advertising professionals operate in an environment where campaign planning consumes significant time yet often falls short on strategic depth. Manual workflows introduce costly errors at every stage, from initial media planning through execution, and the complexity of optimization at scale has grown beyond what traditional approaches can effectively manage. Teams find themselves trapped between the need for sophisticated, data-driven strategies and the operational realities of stitching together fragmented tools, datasets, and decision points. This tension creates bottlenecks that slow time-to-market, erode campaign performance, and limit the strategic value advertising teams can deliver to their organizations.

## Reimagining the Advertising Value Chain Through Agentic AI

The demonstration centers on how agentic AI can fundamentally restructure advertising workflows by introducing autonomous agents capable of reasoning, collaborating, and executing across the entire campaign lifecycle. Rather than treating media planning, audience targeting, bid optimization, and monetization as discrete manual tasks, the agentic approach orchestrates these functions as an interconnected system where specialized agents handle distinct responsibilities while coordinating toward unified campaign objectives. This shift moves advertising from a sequence of human-driven handoffs to a continuously optimizing system that adapts in real time to performance signals and market conditions.

### Building on Amazon Bedrock AgentCore and the Strands SDK

The technical foundation combines Amazon Bedrock AgentCore with the Strands SDK to deliver a robust platform for building and deploying advertising agents. AgentCore provides the runtime and orchestration capabilities needed to support sophisticated agent behaviors, while the Strands SDK accelerates development by giving teams the building blocks to construct agents that reason about complex advertising decisions. Together, these technologies enable the creation of multi-agent systems that can be deployed reliably in production advertising environments where speed, accuracy, and scale all matter.

### Leveraging AdCP Open Standards for Interoperability

A critical element of the architecture is its alignment with AdCP, the open agentic advertising standards. By adopting these standards, the solution ensures that agents can collaborate not only within a single organization's stack but also across the broader advertising ecosystem, including publishers, demand-side platforms, and supply-side platforms. This interoperability prevents vendor lock-in and positions advertising agents to participate in an emerging open marketplace where automated negotiation and execution become the norm.

## Outcomes Across the Campaign Lifecycle

The demonstration walks through the practical impact of agentic collaboration at each stage of the advertising workflow. Agents generate media plans grounded in strategic context, refine audience targeting with greater precision, and optimize bids in real time as performance data flows in. On the monetization side, agents work to maximize ad yield by making continuous adjustments that would be impossible to manage manually. The cumulative result is faster campaign planning cycles, measurably improved ad yield, and a meaningful reduction in operational complexity. Advertising teams reclaim time previously spent on repetitive optimization work and redirect that capacity toward higher-value strategic thinking, while the intelligent automation underneath ensures campaigns continue to perform at their best.

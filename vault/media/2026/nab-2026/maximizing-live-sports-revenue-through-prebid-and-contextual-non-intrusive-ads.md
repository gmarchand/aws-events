# Maximizing Live Sports Revenue Through Prebid and Contextual Non-Intrusive Ads

## Overview and Industry Challenge

Publishers and broadcasters operating in the live sports space face a persistent tension between maximizing advertising revenue and preserving an engaging viewer experience. Traditional ad formats often interrupt the flow of live events, while monetization strategies frequently fail to capture the full value of real-time bidding opportunities. This demonstration addresses both challenges by presenting an integrated AWS-based solution that combines large-scale auction optimization with interactive, contextually relevant advertising experiences designed to feel native to the broadcast.

## The Integrated AWS Solution

### Real-Time Bidding at Scale with Prebid Server and AWS RTB Fabric

The core monetization layer is built on Prebid Server integrated with AWS RTB Fabric, enabling publishers to handle parallel bid requests at massive scale while maintaining direct control over demand connections and bidding data. This architecture supports header bidding workflows in which multiple demand sources compete simultaneously for each ad impression, driving up yield through genuine competitive pressure. Publishers gain full transparency across the auction process, with visibility into how each demand partner participates and performs. By owning the integration directly rather than relying on opaque intermediaries, broadcasters retain ownership of valuable bidding signals and audience data, which translates into stronger negotiating positions and more accurate optimization over time.

### Contextual Shoppable Ads via MediaTailor and InTheGame

Beyond traditional spot advertising, the solution introduces interactive, shoppable ad experiences powered by AWS Elemental MediaTailor in partnership with AWS Partner InTheGame. This combination allows contextual product placements to appear within live sports content in a non-intrusive manner, letting viewers complete purchases instantly through QR codes or companion mobile apps. The shoppable layer opens entirely new revenue streams that operate alongside conventional ad breaks rather than competing with them, while the contextual targeting ensures the products surfaced are relevant to the moment unfolding on screen. The result is an ad experience that enhances rather than disrupts viewer engagement and produces measurable e-commerce outcomes that traditional impression-based advertising cannot match.

## Outcomes and Strategic Benefits

The combined architecture delivers three reinforcing outcomes for broadcasters. Ad yield increases through competitive header bidding executed at scale, new revenue streams emerge from shoppable contextual placements that bypass the limits of fixed spot inventory, and viewer experience improves because advertising becomes more relevant and less intrusive. Crucially, publishers maintain direct control over their demand relationships and the data generated by their auctions, ensuring the long-term strategic value of the solution stays in their hands. Together, these elements position live sports broadcasters to monetize their premium content more effectively while strengthening, rather than eroding, the connection with their audience.

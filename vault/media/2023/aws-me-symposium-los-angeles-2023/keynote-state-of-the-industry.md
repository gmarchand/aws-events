# State of the Industry: AI and ML in Media & Entertainment

## Opening Keynote Overview

In this opening keynote, Chris Blandy, Director of Media & Entertainment Business Development and Strategy at AWS, examines the current state of the Media and Entertainment (M&E) industry through the lens of artificial intelligence and machine learning. Drawing on AWS's deep engagement with broadcasters, studios, streaming platforms, and content creators, Blandy frames the discussion around a critical observation: AI and ML are not new arrivals to media workflows but rather technologies that have been quietly powering industry operations for decades.

## The Long History of AI and ML in Media Workflows

### Decades of Behind-the-Scenes Deployment

Blandy emphasizes that the recent surge of public attention around generative AI obscures a much longer history of intelligent automation in the M&E sector. Machine learning has underpinned content recommendation engines, automated metadata tagging, video transcoding optimization, content moderation, and personalization systems for many years. By highlighting this history, Blandy positions today's wave of innovation not as a disruptive break but as a maturation of capabilities the industry has been steadily building. This framing helps audiences understand that media organizations already have foundational experience and infrastructure to build upon as they adopt newer AI techniques.

### Current Industry Momentum and Transformation

The keynote turns to how M&E companies are now extending these established practices into more transformative use cases. Blandy discusses how AWS customers are applying AI and ML across the full content lifecycle, from pre-production and production through post-production, distribution, and consumer engagement. He addresses how generative AI in particular is reshaping creative workflows, accelerating localization, enabling new forms of personalization, and unlocking value from vast archives of legacy content. Throughout, the emphasis falls on practical outcomes: efficiency gains, cost reduction, faster time to market, and the creation of richer audience experiences.

### Strategic Outlook and the Role of AWS

Closing the state-of-the-industry view, Blandy articulates AWS's strategic perspective on where M&E is heading. He underscores the importance of secure, scalable cloud infrastructure as the foundation for responsible AI adoption, the need for purpose-built services that meet the unique demands of media production and distribution, and the value of partnerships across the ecosystem. The keynote sets the stage for deeper sessions that follow, inviting industry leaders to view AI and ML not as experimental technologies but as core competencies that will define competitive advantage in the years ahead.

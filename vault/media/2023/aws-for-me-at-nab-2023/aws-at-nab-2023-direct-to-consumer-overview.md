# AWS at NAB 2023: Direct-to-Consumer Overview

## Showcasing the Future of Streaming and Personalized Content Delivery

At the National Association of Broadcasters (NAB) Show 2023, Amazon Web Services presented a comprehensive overview of its direct-to-consumer (D2C) and streaming capabilities. The showcase emphasized how modern media companies can leverage AWS technology to reach audiences with tailored content and monetization strategies. By bringing together a portfolio of media services under one umbrella, AWS demonstrated its commitment to supporting broadcasters, content creators, and streaming platforms as they navigate an increasingly competitive and fragmented media landscape.

The exhibition focused on the evolving relationship between content providers and viewers, where personalization and flexibility have become central to audience engagement. AWS positioned its offerings as enablers of these experiences, helping customers build streaming services that can adapt to diverse consumer preferences and business models.

## A Comprehensive Portfolio for Audio and Video Experiences

AWS demonstrated the breadth of options available for delivering content directly to consumers, spanning the full spectrum of monetization models. The showcase highlighted how providers can deliver free ad-supported streaming television (FAST), subscription-based services (SVOD), and advertising-supported video on demand (AVOD) experiences using the AWS platform. This flexibility allows media companies to choose the business model that best fits their audience and content strategy, or to combine multiple approaches within a single service.

Beyond video, the demonstrations extended to audio experiences, recognizing the growing importance of streaming audio content alongside traditional video offerings. By supporting both formats, AWS positions itself as a versatile partner for any media company seeking to engage audiences across different consumption habits and devices.

### Personalized Content and Advertising at Scale

A central theme of the showcase was personalization, both in terms of content recommendations and advertising experiences. AWS demonstrated how its services enable providers to tailor what each viewer sees, from the programs recommended on their home screen to the advertisements inserted into their streams. This personalization is critical in the modern streaming landscape, where audience expectations are shaped by sophisticated algorithms and dynamic content delivery.

### End-to-End OTT Solutions

The presentation also highlighted complete over-the-top (OTT) solutions that bring together every component required to launch and operate a streaming service. By offering all-in-one packages, AWS simplifies the process for customers who want to enter the streaming market without assembling disparate technologies themselves. These solutions integrate AWS Elemental Media Services and Amazon Interactive Video Service (IVS), among other components, to provide a turnkey foundation for content creation, delivery, and monetization, allowing media companies to focus on storytelling and audience growth rather than infrastructure complexity.

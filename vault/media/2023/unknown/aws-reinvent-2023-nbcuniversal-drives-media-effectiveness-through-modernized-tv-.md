# NBCUniversal Drives Media Effectiveness Through Modernized TV

## Session Overview

At AWS re:Invent 2023, NBCUniversal presented its journey to transform television advertising through its One Platform initiative. The session explored how shifting consumer behavior, fragmentation of media across emerging and traditional platforms, and an industry-wide push toward measurable outcomes have introduced significant complexity into the advertising ecosystem. NBCUniversal shared how it has partnered with AWS to address these challenges, combining the cultural reach of premium professionally produced content with modern, scalable media technology to deliver more effective advertising experiences.

## The Modernization Journey with One Platform

### Responding to a Fragmented Media Landscape

NBCUniversal explained that audiences no longer consume content in linear, predictable ways. Viewers move fluidly between broadcast television, streaming services such as Peacock, mobile devices, and connected TVs, forcing advertisers to rethink how they reach consumers at scale. To meet this challenge, NBCUniversal built One Platform as a unified video advertising platform that consolidates inventory, audience data, and campaign management across the company's entire portfolio. This unified approach allows advertisers to plan, buy, and measure campaigns holistically rather than navigating disconnected systems for each distribution channel.

### Building a Cloud-Native Foundation with AWS

The team detailed how collaborating with AWS enabled them to modernize legacy advertising infrastructure and adopt cloud-native architectures capable of supporting massive scale and rapid innovation. By leveraging AWS services for data processing, machine learning, and analytics, NBCUniversal created solutions that ingest and activate audience signals in near real time. This foundation supports advanced capabilities such as audience-based targeting, dynamic ad insertion, and cross-platform frequency management, which are essential for delivering relevant advertising in a converged TV environment.

### Driving Measurable Outcomes for Advertisers

A central theme of the discussion was the industry's growing demand for measurable business outcomes rather than traditional reach-and-frequency metrics. NBCUniversal walked through examples of how One Platform enables advertisers to connect campaigns to tangible results, using data-driven measurement and identity solutions developed with AWS. These tools help brands verify that their messages reach the right consumers efficiently, regardless of where viewing occurs, and provide transparent reporting that ties media investment to performance.

## Key Takeaways and Outcomes

NBCUniversal emphasized several lessons from its modernization journey, including the importance of unifying fragmented systems, investing in cloud-based data infrastructure, and aligning technology decisions with the evolving expectations of advertising customers. The collaboration with AWS has allowed the company to scale efficiently, accelerate the rollout of new advertising products, and position itself as a leader in the convergence of premium content and modern media technology. The session closed with NBCUniversal reaffirming its commitment to helping advertisers reach audiences effectively, efficiently, and at scale across the rapidly evolving television landscape.

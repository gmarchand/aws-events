# Amazon MGM Studios: Building a Next-Generation Studio

## Overview of the Session

At AWS re:Invent 2023, Amazon MGM Studios presented how it has redefined modern film and television production by embracing cloud-native workflows. The session detailed the studio's transformation from traditional production methods to a fully integrated cloud ecosystem powered by AWS, enabling visual storytellers to create at unprecedented scale and quality. By combining virtual production stages, cloud-based editing, and partner-driven solutions, Amazon MGM Studios has positioned itself at the forefront of next-generation content creation.

## Cloud-Powered Production at Scale

### Virtual Production and the Stage Environment

Amazon MGM Studios operates one of the largest virtual production stages in Southern California, where physical sets blend seamlessly with digital environments rendered in real time on massive LED volumes. The cameras on this stage feed directly into cloud-connected pipelines, allowing creative teams to iterate on visuals during principal photography rather than waiting for post-production. This approach compresses traditional production timelines, gives directors immediate feedback on composited shots, and reduces the logistical burden of location shoots. AWS infrastructure underpins the data movement, rendering, and asset management required to keep this complex environment running reliably across departments.

### Edit in the Cloud and 8K Workflows

A central pillar of the studio's modernization effort is the adoption of the Edit in the Cloud on AWS solution, deployed within a new facility purpose-built to handle 8K media for both feature films and episodic series. Editors, colorists, and visual effects artists access high-resolution content through secure remote workstations rather than relying on physical media or on-premises storage arrays. This eliminates the friction of moving terabytes of footage between facilities, allows distributed creative teams to collaborate in near real time, and gives the studio the elasticity to scale compute and storage up or down based on production demand. The 8K capability future-proofs the pipeline as delivery requirements continue to grow across streaming and theatrical formats.

### Partner Ecosystem and Creative Empowerment

Amazon MGM Studios extends the value of its AWS foundation through close collaboration with AWS Partner Solutions, integrating specialized tools for editorial, VFX, color grading, and asset management into a coherent end-to-end workflow. By relying on a curated partner ecosystem rather than building every component in-house, the studio gives its artists access to industry-leading creative software while AWS handles the underlying performance, security, and global accessibility. The outcome is a production environment that empowers visual storytellers to focus on craft, accelerates the path from script to screen, and establishes a blueprint for how major studios can operate in a cloud-first era.

## Key Outcomes and Takeaways

The session made clear that Amazon MGM Studios views the cloud not as a back-office utility but as a creative engine. By unifying virtual production, cloud editing, and partner integrations on AWS, the studio has shortened production cycles, expanded collaboration across geographies, and raised the technical ceiling for the content it delivers. The presentation positioned this approach as a model for the broader media and entertainment industry, demonstrating that next-generation storytelling depends as much on cloud architecture as it does on cameras and lenses.

# Carrier's Abound Platform: Transforming Connected Buildings Through Cloud Innovation

## Overview of Carrier's Digital Transformation

Carrier, a global leader in heating, ventilation, air conditioning, and refrigeration solutions, has undertaken a significant strategic transformation to evolve beyond traditional equipment manufacturing into a provider of digital products and climate solutions. This shift reflects a broader industry recognition that modern buildings generate vast amounts of operational data that, when properly harnessed, can deliver substantial value to building operators, owners, and occupants. By embracing cloud-native architectures and digital innovation, Carrier has positioned itself to address the growing demand for intelligent, sustainable building management systems that respond to evolving energy efficiency, occupant wellness, and operational performance requirements.

The centerpiece of this transformation is the Abound platform, a connected building solution designed to unify the fragmented data landscape that typically characterizes commercial buildings. Building operators have historically struggled with disparate systems—HVAC, lighting, security, and air quality monitoring among them—each producing isolated streams of information that resist meaningful integration. Abound addresses this challenge directly by serving as a centralized layer that aggregates building data and translates it into actionable insights operators can use to improve performance, reduce costs, and enhance occupant experiences.

## Building the Abound Platform with Eviden

### Strategic Partnership and Collaborative Delivery

The development and ongoing evolution of Abound reflects a deep collaboration between Carrier and Eviden, an AWS Partner that brings specialized expertise in cloud engineering, IoT, and digital platform development. Rather than approaching the platform as a vendor-client engagement, the two organizations have worked as integrated partners, jointly shaping the technical architecture and product roadmap. This collaborative model has allowed Carrier to accelerate its digital product strategy by leveraging Eviden's engineering capabilities while maintaining domain leadership in building systems and climate solutions.

### Tailored Solutions for Building Operators

A central theme of the partnership is the ability to deliver customized solutions that meet the specific requirements of mutual customers. Buildings vary widely in their equipment mix, operational priorities, and regulatory contexts, so a rigid, one-size-fits-all platform would fail to serve the diverse market Carrier addresses. The Abound platform, supported by Eviden's delivery capabilities, instead offers flexibility that allows building operators to focus on the insights most relevant to their environments—whether those involve indoor air quality, energy consumption optimization, predictive maintenance, or space utilization.

### Driving Innovation Through Centralized Data

By consolidating building data on a unified cloud platform built on AWS, Abound creates the foundation for ongoing innovation. Centralized data enables advanced analytics, machine learning applications, and the rapid development of new features that would be impractical in siloed environments. This architectural choice positions Carrier to introduce pioneering solutions over time, expanding the platform's value as additional capabilities and integrations are layered on top of the core data foundation.

## Outcomes and Partnership Value

The collaboration between Carrier and Eviden demonstrates how established industrial companies can successfully reinvent themselves as digital solution providers when paired with the right technology partner and cloud foundation. The Abound platform delivers tangible value to building operators by transforming previously disconnected data into operational intelligence, while the Carrier-Eviden partnership solidifies a delivery model capable of scaling to meet diverse customer needs. The outcome reinforces both companies' positioning in the connected buildings market and establishes a template for continued innovation as the demand for intelligent, sustainable buildings accelerates.

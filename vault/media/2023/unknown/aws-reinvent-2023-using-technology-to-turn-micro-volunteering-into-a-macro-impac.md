# Using Technology to Turn Micro-Volunteering into Macro Impact

## Addressing the Global Language Barrier in Humanitarian Crises

The forcibly displaced population worldwide has grown to exceed 100 million people, creating an urgent humanitarian challenge that extends far beyond shelter and food. A staggering 44 percent of refugees cannot understand the information they receive or communicate effectively with the people trying to help them. This communication gap manifests in refugee camps, during legal processing, and throughout resettlement in host countries, often determining whether individuals can access healthcare, secure asylum, or rebuild their lives. Tarjimly was founded to confront this systemic barrier by democratizing humanitarian language access through a combination of community mobilization and modern technology.

## The Tarjimly Model and Its Technological Foundation

### Mobilizing Volunteers Through an On-Demand Platform

Tarjimly operates a mobile platform that connects refugees and humanitarian workers with bilingual volunteers in real time. Rather than asking individuals for long-term commitments, the organization embraces micro-volunteering, allowing thousands of translators around the world to contribute brief moments of their time whenever they are available. This approach lowers the barrier to participation and unlocks a vast, distributed pool of linguistic talent that can serve hundreds of thousands of refugees globally. By aggregating these small acts of service, Tarjimly transforms individually modest contributions into a sustained, large-scale humanitarian response.

### Applying AI and Machine Learning to Match Needs with Skills

The core of Tarjimly's technical solution lies in its use of AI and machine learning, supported by AWS infrastructure, to intelligently match translators with refugees and humanitarian workers. The matching system considers language pairs, availability, and contextual needs to ensure that requests are routed to the most suitable volunteer as quickly as possible. By automating what would otherwise be a logistically impossible coordination challenge, the platform delivers responsive, scalable language access across a wide variety of humanitarian situations.

### Scaling Impact Through Community and Technology

The combination of an engaged volunteer community and a robust technology backbone allows Tarjimly to scale its mission far beyond what traditional translation services could achieve. The platform demonstrates how cloud-based tools and machine learning can amplify the reach of nonprofit organizations, turning a fragmented global volunteer base into a coordinated force capable of meaningful, measurable humanitarian impact. The outcome is a model in which technology does not replace human compassion but multiplies it, enabling communication, dignity, and access for displaced people at a global scale.

# AWS Public Sector Symposium New Delhi 2023: Keynote Summary

## Driving India's Digital Transformation Through Cloud

The keynote at the AWS Public Sector Symposium New Delhi 2023, delivered by Shalini Kapoor, leader of public sector and chief technologist for India at AWS, centered on the pivotal role cloud services play in accelerating India's digital economy. Kapoor emphasized that cloud computing has become indispensable for government agencies, educational institutions, nonprofits, and enterprises seeking to modernize service delivery, scale operations, and innovate at speed. She positioned India not merely as a consumer of global cloud innovations, but as an emerging hub where solutions are designed locally and replicated globally to address complex challenges in areas such as healthcare, education, governance, and space exploration.

Throughout the address, Kapoor underscored AWS's long-term commitment to India through continued infrastructure investment, skills development programs, and partnerships with both public and private sector institutions. The keynote reinforced the message that digital transformation is no longer optional for organizations aiming to remain competitive and relevant in a rapidly evolving economy.

### Strategic Announcements and Partnerships

A major highlight of the keynote was the announcement that AWS has signed a strategic Memorandum of Understanding with the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) and IN-SPACe, the Indian National Space Promotion and Authorization Center. This collaboration is designed to support India's growing space ecosystem by providing cloud-based capabilities to space-tech startups, researchers, and government agencies, enabling faster experimentation, large-scale data processing, and the commercialization of space technologies. The partnership signals AWS's intent to deeply embed itself in India's strategic technology priorities and demonstrates how cloud infrastructure can underpin national missions of significant scientific and economic importance.

## Asia Pacific as the Engine of Global Growth

Rahul Sharma, regional managing director for worldwide public sector in Asia Pacific and Japan at AWS, joined the stage to provide a broader regional perspective. Sharma identified Asia Pacific as the largest driver of global growth in 2023 and outlined AWS's sustained investment commitments across the region. He encouraged customers to push the boundaries of what is possible by leveraging the breadth of AWS services, ranging from foundational compute and storage to advanced capabilities in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and analytics. Sharma's remarks framed cloud adoption as a catalyst not only for organizational efficiency but also for regional economic competitiveness and societal impact.

### Customer Stories: EkStep Foundation and Madhya Pradesh

To ground the strategic vision in practical outcomes, Kapoor and Sharma were joined on stage by Dr. Pramod Varma, chief technology officer of the EkStep Foundation, and Shri Abhijeet Agrawal, managing director of the Madhya Pradesh State Electronics Development Corporation under the Department of Science and Technology, Government of Madhya Pradesh.

Dr. Varma shared how EkStep Foundation has leveraged AWS to build scalable digital public goods that have transformed learning outcomes for millions of children across India. He highlighted the importance of open, interoperable digital infrastructure and how cloud elasticity has enabled the foundation to reach underserved communities at population scale without compromising performance or cost efficiency.

Shri Abhijeet Agrawal discussed how the Madhya Pradesh state government is using AWS to accelerate digital transformation across citizen services, drive productivity within government departments, and stimulate economic development at the state level. His remarks illustrated how state-level public sector entities can adopt cloud strategically to deliver measurable improvements in governance, transparency, and service quality for citizens.

## Outcomes and Forward Outlook

The keynote collectively conveyed a clear message: India is positioned as a global hub of cloud-driven innovation, and AWS is committed to enabling that trajectory through infrastructure, partnerships, and ecosystem support. The announcements regarding ISRO and IN-SPACe, combined with the customer testimonials from EkStep and Madhya Pradesh, demonstrated that cloud adoption in India spans the full spectrum from cutting-edge space research to grassroots education and state governance. The session closed with an implicit call to action for public sector leaders to embrace cloud as the foundation for inclusive growth, scalable innovation, and the realization of India's broader digital economy ambitions.

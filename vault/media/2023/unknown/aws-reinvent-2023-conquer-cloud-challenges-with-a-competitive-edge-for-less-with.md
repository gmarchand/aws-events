# Conquering Cloud Challenges with AMD EPYC on AWS

## Session Overview

This AWS re:Invent 2023 session, presented by AMD as an AWS Partner, explores how organizations can gain a competitive edge in the cloud while reducing infrastructure costs through the strategic adoption of AMD EPYC CPU-powered Amazon EC2 instances. The presentation emphasizes that CPU choice is a critical decision that directly impacts both performance outcomes and financial efficiency in cloud deployments. AMD experts guide attendees through the technical and economic considerations of selecting the right processor architecture for their workloads, demonstrating how thoughtful CPU selection can free up budget for value-driven initiatives rather than baseline infrastructure spending.

## Performance and Efficiency Advantages of AMD EPYC

### Architectural Strengths Driving Workload Performance

The session highlights the cutting-edge capabilities of AMD EPYC processors, positioning them as best-in-class options for performance and efficiency in cloud environments. AMD EPYC CPUs are engineered to deliver strong throughput, scalability, and energy efficiency, making them well-suited for demanding enterprise workloads as well as continuously running applications where optimized hourly rates matter most. Whether customers prioritize peak performance for compute-intensive tasks or seek consistent value for steady-state operations, AMD demonstrates that there is an EPYC-powered EC2 instance type aligned to those specific requirements.

### Translating Technical Gains into Business Value

Beyond raw performance metrics, the presentation focuses on how the efficiency gains of AMD EPYC translate into meaningful business outcomes. By achieving comparable or superior performance at lower cost points, organizations can strategically reallocate cloud infrastructure budgets toward innovation, modernization, and growth initiatives. This reframing positions CPU selection not merely as a technical choice but as a financial lever that influences an organization's ability to invest in its strategic priorities.

## Strategic Guidance for Cloud Decision Makers

### Navigating the EC2 Instance Landscape

AMD experts walk attendees through the breadth of EC2 instance families powered by AMD EPYC processors, helping technical leaders match workload characteristics to the most appropriate instance options. The guidance emphasizes evaluating applications based on their performance profiles, scalability needs, and runtime patterns to ensure that infrastructure decisions deliver both technical fit and cost optimization.

### Elevating Technical and Financial Acumen

The overarching takeaway is that conquering cloud challenges requires combining technical understanding with financial discipline. By leveraging AMD EPYC CPUs on AWS, organizations can achieve a competitive edge through lower total costs while maintaining or improving the performance their applications demand. The session encourages attendees to treat CPU selection as a deliberate, strategic decision and provides them with the framework needed to evaluate AMD-powered options against their unique workload and budget requirements.

# Running Sustainable Real-Time Advertising Workloads in the Cloud

## Session Overview

This AWS re:Invent 2023 session, designated ADM302, addressed technology and engineering leaders working with high-scale advertising technology workloads. The presentation explored how organizations operating real-time bidding platforms, ad exchanges, and programmatic advertising systems can leverage AWS cloud infrastructure to simultaneously reduce operational costs, optimize performance at massive scale, and lower their carbon footprint. The central theme positioned sustainability not as a trade-off against performance or cost, but as a complementary objective that aligns naturally with sound architectural design.

## Cloud-Driven Sustainability for Ad Tech

### Balancing Performance, Cost, and Carbon Footprint

Real-time advertising workloads present unique challenges due to their extreme scale, low-latency requirements, and unpredictable traffic patterns. The session examined how companies running these demanding workloads are turning to the cloud to achieve efficiencies that would be difficult or impossible to replicate in traditional on-premises environments. By taking advantage of elastic scaling, modern compute options, and managed services, organizations can match resource consumption more precisely to actual demand, eliminating the wasteful over-provisioning common in legacy ad tech stacks.

### Embedding Sustainability into Architectural Design

A core message of the session was that sustainability should be considered a first-class architectural concern rather than an afterthought. Engineering leaders were encouraged to bring environmental considerations into the design process from the outset, evaluating choices such as instance selection, regional placement, data storage tiering, and workload scheduling through a sustainability lens. The discussion highlighted how decisions that reduce energy consumption frequently align with decisions that reduce cost and improve performance, creating a virtuous cycle for ad tech organizations willing to rethink their infrastructure approach.

### Practical Outcomes for Ad Tech Companies

The session illustrated how companies in the advertising ecosystem are achieving measurable improvements by adopting cloud-native designs optimized for both efficiency and environmental impact. By migrating and modernizing real-time bidding and ad serving workloads on AWS, these organizations are reducing the carbon intensity of every ad transaction while supporting the throughput and latency demands their businesses require. The outcome reinforces a broader industry trend in which sustainability commitments become achievable through the same architectural modernization efforts that drive competitive advantage.

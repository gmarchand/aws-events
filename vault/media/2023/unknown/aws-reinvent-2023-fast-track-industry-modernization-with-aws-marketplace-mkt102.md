# Fast-Tracking Industry Modernization with AWS Marketplace

## Overview of the Session

This AWS re:Invent 2023 session explores how organizations across industries are accelerating their modernization journeys by leveraging specialized partner solutions available through AWS Marketplace. As cloud-based, industry-focused solutions proliferate, business leaders, technical decision-makers, and line-of-business executives increasingly favor a "buy versus build" strategy. They seek purpose-built offerings tailored to the distinct demands of their sectors rather than investing the time and resources required to develop comparable capabilities internally. AWS Marketplace serves as the central hub where these decision-makers can discover, evaluate, and procure industry-specific software, data products, and services from a vetted ecosystem of partners.

## Lionsgate's Cloud Modernization Journey

### Embracing Industry Partner Solutions

The session features global entertainment leader Lionsgate, which shares its experience moving to the cloud and modernizing its operations through AWS. Rather than building every capability in-house, Lionsgate turned to specialized partners whose solutions were purpose-built for the media and entertainment industry. By tapping into this ecosystem, the company was able to address sector-specific challenges—such as content management, distribution, and analytics—without diverting engineering resources from core creative and business priorities.

### Simplifying Procurement to Unlock Innovation

A central theme of Lionsgate's experience is the role AWS Marketplace played in streamlining procurement. Traditional software acquisition processes involve lengthy vendor evaluations, contract negotiations, and billing complexities that can stall innovation. AWS Marketplace consolidated these steps into a unified procurement experience, allowing Lionsgate to discover qualified industry partners, accelerate contracting, and consolidate billing through their existing AWS relationship. This streamlined approach shortened time-to-value and enabled the company to deploy new capabilities faster than would have been possible through conventional channels.

### Lessons Learned Along the Way

Lionsgate's representatives share practical lessons drawn from their modernization journey, emphasizing the importance of aligning partner selection with specific industry use cases, leveraging Marketplace as a strategic procurement tool rather than just a transactional one, and engaging cross-functional stakeholders early to ensure that purchased solutions integrate smoothly into existing workflows. Their experience underscores that successful modernization depends not only on technology choices but also on operational and procurement practices that enable speed and agility.

## Key Takeaways for Decision-Makers

The session concludes by reinforcing that industry modernization is best achieved through a combination of cloud infrastructure and specialized partner expertise. AWS Marketplace functions as the connective tissue that links enterprises to a curated ecosystem of industry-relevant solutions, simplifying discovery and procurement while accelerating the path to innovation. For organizations weighing build-versus-buy decisions, the Lionsgate story offers a tangible example of how leveraging partner solutions through Marketplace can free internal teams to focus on differentiated work while still meeting unique industry requirements.

# AWS Partner Network Technical Validations: Growing Your Practice and Delighting Customers

## Overview of the Session

This AWS re:Invent 2023 session (PEX204) is designed for AWS Partners seeking to differentiate their solutions through AWS technical validation programs. The presentation addresses how partners can strengthen their offerings, qualify for prestigious AWS programs, and ultimately deliver greater value to their customers. By focusing on the most frequently encountered challenges during the validation process, the session equips partners with actionable insights to refine their software products, consulting practices, and managed services in alignment with AWS best practices.

## Addressing Common Partner Challenges

### Identifying Gaps in Solutions and Offerings

The session explores the recurring obstacles that AWS Partners face when constructing software solutions or consulting offers intended for technical validation. These challenges typically involve gaps in architectural design, security posture, operational readiness, and adherence to AWS Well-Architected principles. Recognizing these shortcomings early in the development process allows partners to address them proactively rather than discovering them during a formal review, saving both time and resources while accelerating the path to validation.

### Improving Products and Practices

Once gaps are identified, the focus shifts to remediation strategies that elevate the maturity of partner offerings. Partners learn how to refine their software products to meet AWS technical standards, mature their consulting methodologies to demonstrate consistent customer outcomes, and enhance their service delivery models. This holistic improvement not only prepares partners for validation but also strengthens their overall market positioning and credibility with customers seeking trusted AWS expertise.

## Pathways to AWS Competency and Service Delivery Programs

### Joining the Programs

The session provides detailed guidance on the technical best practices required to qualify for the AWS Competency Program and AWS Service Delivery Program. These programs serve as powerful differentiators, signaling to customers that a partner has demonstrated deep expertise in specific technology domains, industry verticals, or AWS services. Achieving these designations opens doors to co-selling opportunities, enhanced visibility in AWS Partner directories, and stronger relationships with AWS field teams.

### Completing the Required Validations

Practical tips for successfully completing the required validations form a central theme of the session. Partners gain insight into how to prepare documentation, structure architectural reviews, and demonstrate the operational rigor that AWS reviewers expect. By understanding the evaluation criteria upfront and aligning their submissions accordingly, partners can streamline the validation process and avoid common pitfalls that delay approval.

## Key Outcomes for Partners

Attendees leave the session with a clear understanding of how to position their solutions for technical validation success, the specific best practices needed to meet AWS program requirements, and a roadmap for continuous improvement of their AWS practices. The ultimate outcome is twofold: partners grow their businesses through differentiated offerings and program designations, while their customers benefit from validated, high-quality solutions that have been independently verified against AWS standards.

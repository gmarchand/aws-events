# AWS re:Invent 2023 - Taking BMW Group’s in-vehicle voice experience from idea to reality (ALX201)

[Video Link](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mFbt7TfoI_o)

## Description

In this session, learn how BMW Group is building a unique, next-generation AI voice assistant that takes the BMW and MINI in-vehicle voice experience to the next level. With or without an internet connection, AWS expands the BMW cloud environment by deploying an embedded, neural text-to-speech SDK that provides a highly natural, uninterrupted voice experience. This session explores BMW Group’s vision of in-car voice assistants. Learn from their insights into the entire product development journey from ideation, distilling brand personality traits, recruiting voice actors, and training large multilingual speech models to road testing and, ultimately, launching the branded BMW and MINI Intelligent Personal Assistant using Amazon Alexa and AWS infrastructure.

# AWS re:Invent 2025 - Managing Amazon EC2 capacity and availability (CMP331)

[Video Link](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=soZfsq46bbA)

## Description

Amazon EC2 offers a variety of capacity usage and reservation models, so you can choose the right combination for your workload and budget. These include capacity reservations, On-Demand Instances, Amazon EC2 Capacity Blocks for ML, and more. Join this session to learn how to combine these models in a way that's best for your business. You'll walk away with knowledge on how to manage your capacity to improve utilization and availability.

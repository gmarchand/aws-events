# AWS re:Invent 2025 - Transforming Integrated Diagnostics: Philips' AI Journey with AWS (IND219)

[Video Link](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y8g9gY9pOzY)

## Description

Healthcare providers struggle with disconnected patient data sources across diagnostic platforms, hampering clinical decision-making. Philips addresses this through integrated cloud-based solutions in radiology, cardiology, and digital pathology, powered by AI capabilities. As digital pathology emerges as the next innovation frontier, cloud infrastructure becomes crucial for managing massive imaging datasets and enabling collaboration.Incorporating AWS HealthImaging and AWS generative AI tools, Philips has developed comprehensive digital pathology solutions that streamline workflows, enhance diagnostic accuracy, and enables global collaboration. The goal is to improve diagnosis and treatment planning through standardized DICOM implementation, AI-assisted analysis, and seamless data sharing across healthcare institutions worldwide.

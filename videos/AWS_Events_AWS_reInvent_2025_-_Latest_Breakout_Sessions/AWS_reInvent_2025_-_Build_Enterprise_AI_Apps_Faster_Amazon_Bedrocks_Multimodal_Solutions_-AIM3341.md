## Description

Enterprise developers face challenges processing diverse unstructured content across multiple formats, often requiring complex integration of various AI services. Learn how Amazon Bedrock Data Automation simplifies AI application development by unifying workflows for documents, audio, images, and video into a single service. Through Air's success story discover how Bedrock Data Automation helps streamline content processing, optimizes price-performance, and enables customized insights generation. See how teams can accelerate their journey from experimentation to production while maintaining flexibility to use existing AI services where needed.

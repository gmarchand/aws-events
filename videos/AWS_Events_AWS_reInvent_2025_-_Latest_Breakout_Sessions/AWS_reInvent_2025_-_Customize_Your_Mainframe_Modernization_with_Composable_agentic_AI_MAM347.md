## Description

Master custom mainframe modernization with composable architecture. See how enterprise leaders are accelerating their modernization journeys by integrating specialized partner tools directly into AWS workflows with the power of agentic AI. Learn how this composable approach enables customized solutions for even the most complex mainframe environments, and discover how to craft your optimized modernization workflow using AWS services integrated with trusted partner solutions.

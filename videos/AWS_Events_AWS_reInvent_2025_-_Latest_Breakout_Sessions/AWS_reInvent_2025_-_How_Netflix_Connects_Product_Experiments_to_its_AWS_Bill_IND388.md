# AWS re:Invent 2025 - How Netflix Connects Product Experiments to its AWS Bill (IND388)

[Video Link](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ulFeSnJlRvk)

## Description

How do you measure the cost of innovation? At Netflix, a "winning" A/B test can create hidden cloud infrastructure costs that offset its business value. This session details our strategy for connecting product experiments to their infrastructure costs. We'll share our framework for identifying an experiment's impact on our cloud systems and show how we translate that impact into a projected AWS cost. Learn how this enables our teams to make smarter, more cost-aware product decisions at scale.

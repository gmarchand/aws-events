# AWS re:Invent 2025 - How Netflix Shapes our Fleet for Efficiency and Reliability (IND387)

[Video Link](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K-2u50e0VzA)

## Description

Netflix's strategy for optimizing its cloud architecture for efficiency and reliability. Placement of workloads on price-optimal hardware by automatically modeling capacity requirements and understanding AWS hardware performance and pricing. The talk will also cover a continuous optimization loop that monitors and reshapes the fleet to maintain business outcomes as load patterns change. Finally, it will touch upon managing traffic demand and compute supply to ensure availability during unexpected load shifts.

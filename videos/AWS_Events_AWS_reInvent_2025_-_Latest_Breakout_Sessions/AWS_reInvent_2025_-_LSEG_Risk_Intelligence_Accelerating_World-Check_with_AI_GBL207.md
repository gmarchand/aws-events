## Description

LSEG’s Risk Intelligence business offers various solutions in the risk screening, digital identity and due diligence space to Financial Institutions. The Content Curation AI project is focused on improving data services which rely on manual processes to analyse and identify media coverage of individuals or companies engaged in risky behaviour. The goal is to automate, ingest and analyse information, alleviating tactical burden on staff and in doing so, free up human resources to focus on higher level value add contributions. Thus increasing LSEG's competitive advantage in the product marketplace. Programme running live in production and being run with AWS, LSEG and MeshAI.

# AWS re:Invent 2025 - Capital One: Building resilient systems by engineering for five nines (IND3308)

[Video Link](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aS8sqMUd5A8)

## Description

99.999% availability—less than five minutes of downtime per year—is the gold standard for mission-critical applications. This session dives deep into architectural strategies to achieve such availability using AWS. We'll explore fault-tolerant patterns such as multi-Region deployments, active-active failover, circuit breakers, bulkheads, chaos testing, and event-driven designs. Learn how services like Amazon S3, Amazon DynamoDB Global Tables, and AWS Lambda can eliminate single points of failure. We’ll cover SLAs, SLOs, and engineering observability and automation to detect and respond to failures within milliseconds. Case studies and practical design guidance make this session ideal for architects and engineers building for extreme reliability.

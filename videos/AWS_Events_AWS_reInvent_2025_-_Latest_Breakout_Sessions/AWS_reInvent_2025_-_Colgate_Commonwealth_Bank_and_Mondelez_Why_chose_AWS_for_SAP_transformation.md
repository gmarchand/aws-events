# AWS re:Invent 2025 - Colgate, Commonwealth Bank and Mondelez: Why chose AWS for SAP transformation

[Video Link](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3pb_-CaVe6Q)

## Description

For decades, SAP has been the business process backbone for leading companies across nearly every industry; and thousands of customers trust AWS to run SAP.Many customers who are still running their SAP applications on-premises, and their impending SAP migration is part of a broader transformation they are undertaking – where SAP is a critical application, but just one of the components of something bigger. In this session, customers like Colagate, Commonwealth Bank of Australia, and Mondelez will share their transformation journeys including moving SAP applications on AWS, and  how they are partnering with Amazon to chart the future for their organization.

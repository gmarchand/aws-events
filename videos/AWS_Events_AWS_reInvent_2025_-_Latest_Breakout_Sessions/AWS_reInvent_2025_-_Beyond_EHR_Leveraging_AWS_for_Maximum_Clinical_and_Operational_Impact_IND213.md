# AWS re:Invent 2025 - Beyond EHR: Leveraging AWS for Maximum Clinical and Operational Impact (IND213)

## Description

Successful EHR hosting on AWS has become standard practice, as evidenced by AWS' 2025 Best in KLAS rating for public cloud. This session features two innovative health systems demonstrating how they've moved beyond basic EHR implementation to create competitive advantages with AWS. Leaders will share strategies for moving past "lift-and-shift" to drive meaningful innovation by leveraging cloud-native solutions, extending EHR functionality, and developing unique patient and provider experiences. Through real-world examples, attendees will learn practical approaches to healthcare innovation, value creation frameworks, and implementation insights. Designed for technology leaders, digital strategists, and clinical innovation teams seeking to differentiate their organizations.

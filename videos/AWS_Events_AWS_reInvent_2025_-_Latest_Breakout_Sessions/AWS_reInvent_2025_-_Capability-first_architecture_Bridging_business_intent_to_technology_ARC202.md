# AWS re:Invent 2025 - Capability-first architecture: Bridging business intent to technology (ARC202)

[Video Link](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GtBFC27Buc8)

## Description

With 200+ AWS services and a growing ecosystem of third-party solutions, making the right architectural choices can feel overwhelming. This session introduces a capability-first approach for moving from business goals to technology choices in a structured manner. Through practical examples, learn how this methodology narrows the solution space and keeps architectures adaptable to change. Discover how to make strategic, capability-aligned decisions that scale with your business, whether designing new solutions or refactoring legacy systems.

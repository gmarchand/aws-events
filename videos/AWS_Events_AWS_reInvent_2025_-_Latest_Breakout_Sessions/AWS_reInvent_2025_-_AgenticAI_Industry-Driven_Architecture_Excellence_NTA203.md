## Description

Discover how industry-specific requirements shape architectural patterns for effective Agentic AI implementations. This session explores how tailored architectural frameworks address unique challenges in healthcare, finance, manufacturing, and logistics. Through real-world use cases, learn the critical design considerations that drive innovation across sectors. Attendees will gain practical knowledge of implementation strategies that unlock Agentic AI's transformative potential, enabling them to apply these architectural patterns within their own industries.

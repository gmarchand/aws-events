# AWS re:Invent 2025 - Dive deep into Amazon DynamoDB (DAT435)

[Video Link](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B05YpQ089w8)

## Description

This session provides a deep dive into Amazon DynamoDB and shares insights into how DynamoDB is architected and how it’s able to deliver the scalability and response times that customers have come to expect. DynamoDB is used by over one million AWS customers, and powers hundreds of applications that exceed half a million requests per second. In this talk we explore the architecture of DynamoDB, and of several specific features. You will understand how we architected DynamoDB to deliver predictable low latency at any scale, and how best to develop your own applications to delight your customers.

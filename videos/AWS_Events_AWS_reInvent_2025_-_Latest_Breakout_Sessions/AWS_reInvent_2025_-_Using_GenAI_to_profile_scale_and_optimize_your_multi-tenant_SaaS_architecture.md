# AWS re:Invent 2025 - Using GenAI to profile, scale, and optimize your multi-tenant SaaS architecture

[Video Link](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vcYT6INWpa4)

## Description

SaaS builders have always relied heavily on metrics and profiling data to assess the real-time profile of their SaaS architecture. How efficiently the system is consuming resources, how tenants are applying loads, where noisy neighbor conditions are surfacing—these are amongst a long list of insights that SaaS architects rely on. This session will look at how GenAI can be brought to this experience, enabling builders to get richer views into how their multi-tenant strategies and policies are working. GenAI opens all new paths into interrogating the footprint of a SaaS environment, including analyzing key experience insights (time to value, throttling patters, over-provisioning, etc.). The goal is to look at how/where these GenAI mechanisms can be introduced into your architecture and how they can be analyzed with various GenAI tools.

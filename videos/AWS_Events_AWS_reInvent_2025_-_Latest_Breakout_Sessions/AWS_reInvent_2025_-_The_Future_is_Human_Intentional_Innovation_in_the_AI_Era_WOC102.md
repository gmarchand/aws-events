## Description

The pace of change in technology and the cloud demands human-centered leadership, grounded in intention and inclusion. Today’s leaders must deliver progress at speed while earning trust and creating cultures where both people and ideas can thrive in the future of work. This session brings together women driving transformation in the cloud to share how intentional leadership requires listening deeply, fostering confidence, and navigating uncertainty. Learn the priorities that drive meaningful impact and enable innovation in today’s rapidly evolving landscape. Moderated by Christina Mitine, Sr. Go-To-Market Lead. The panel will feature AWS Customers.

## Description

In a year defined by economic pressure and rapid AI change, developer experience (DevEx) is more critical than ever. Join us as we unveil key insights from Atlassian’s State of DevEx 2025 Report, including how AI and AI agents are reshaping workflows, boosting collaboration, and freeing developers for higher-value work. We’ll explore critical challenges: a widening gap between leaders and developers, and rising inefficiencies costing 50% of devs 10+ hours each week. Walk away with fresh data and actionable ideas to elevate DevEx in your organization. This presentation is brought to you by Atlassian, an AWS Partner.

# AWS re:Invent 2025 - Cochlear enhances contact center observability with Amazon Connect (BIZ215)

[Video Link](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xWVhvJuhfdU)

## Description

Real-time visibility of contact center operations and customer interactions is critical to delivering excellent customer service. In this session, hear from Cochlear — the global leader in implantable hearing solutions — on how you can leverage Amazon Connect to observe and manage end-to-end customer experience operations, from forecasting contacts, to planning workforce schedules, to delivering human agent coaching, and beyond. Learn how Amazon Connect puts the power of AI in your hands to deliver end-to-end observability in your contact center.

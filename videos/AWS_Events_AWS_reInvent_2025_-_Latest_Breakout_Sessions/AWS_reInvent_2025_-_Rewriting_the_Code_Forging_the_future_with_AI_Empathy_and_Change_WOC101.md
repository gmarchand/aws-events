# AWS re:Invent 2025 - Rewriting the Code: Forging the future with AI, Empathy and Change (WOC101)

[Video Link](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gvz991i9PR4)

## Description

Tech can change the world; empathy ensures it changes for the better. This session examines the critical intersection of generative AI, emotional intelligence, and women's unique role in shaping technology's future. Leaders and change makers are not just participants in the digital revolution—they are architects of more inclusive workplaces, industries, and communities. AWS is at the forefront of this transformation, implementing robust governance frameworks for responsible AI development. Women technologists at AWS are leading initiatives to ensure AI systems are trained on a variety of datasets and incorporate inclusive design principles from the ground up. Participants will gain perspectives on how AI-driven solutions can reduce bias, amplify voices, and create pathways for equitable progress. Let’s rewrite the code! This session is sponsored by Deloitte.

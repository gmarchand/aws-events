# AWS re:Invent 2025 - FinOps 3.0: Cost Intelligence for the AI Era (AIM268)

[Video Link](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2SP0eOXqOqc)

## Description

FinOps has evolved from visibility to optimization and now to action. As AI workloads introduce new cost complexities, organizations can no longer afford analysis paralysis or endless dashboards. It is time for FinOps 3.0: a shift where cost intelligence is embedded directly into engineering workflows, insights are contextual and role specific, and automation becomes a trusted ally rather than a leap of faith. This session explores how to apply lessons from Kubernetes cost management to today’s AI challenges and provides a practical roadmap to help teams innovate faster, smarter, and more sustainably in the cloud.

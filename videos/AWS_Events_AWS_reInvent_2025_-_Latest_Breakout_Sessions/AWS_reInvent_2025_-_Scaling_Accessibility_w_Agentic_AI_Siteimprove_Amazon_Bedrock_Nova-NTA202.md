# AWS re:Invent 2025 - Scaling Accessibility w/ Agentic AI: Siteimprove + Amazon Bedrock & Nova-NTA202

[Video Link](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_gnj3ornavE)

## Description

Discover how Siteimprove, a market-leader providing an agentic content intelligence platform for digital accessibility and content performance, harnesses Amazon Bedrock, Bedrock Agents and Nova to create accessible digital experiences. This session takes you from concept to production, showcasing how Siteimprove partnered with the AWS GenAI Innovation Center to scale accessibility assessments to millions of requests each month while improving accuracy and cutting costs. See how these advancements are helping customers deliver accessible, compliant, and user-friendly websites—expanding audience reach and meeting regulatory requirements with confidence. You’ll walk away with best practices for project planning, resource optimization, and agentic AI implementation, plus actionable strategies to scale your own AI-powered accessibility solutions on AWS—no matter your starting point.

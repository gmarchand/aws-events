## Description

Is your organization operating in a multicloud environment? Drawing from thousands of executive and builder engagements, we will cut through the noise with practical guidance to help you succeed with your multicloud strategy. In this session, we will describe the AWS multicloud approach that aims to help you build multicloud capabilities across People, Process and Technology pillars. You will learn how to build a business case-driven multicloud strategy, establishing centralized management and oversight of your multicloud environment and architectural best practices for multicloud.

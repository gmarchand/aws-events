# AWS re:Invent 2025 - So Energy streamlines customer service with AI-powered automation (BIZ211)

## Description

Legacy contact center solutions weren’t built for today’s customer expectations. Managing fragmented voice, chat, and email systems creates friction for administrators, agents, and customers alike. Join AWS and So Energy — a UK-based renewable energy provider — to learn how you can modernise ageing customer service experiences by consolidating disjointed channels into a single, unified application with Amazon Connect. Leveraging AI-powered automation and deep CRM integration, learn how So Energy agents gained a single view of the customer journey, enabling them to resolve issues faster and reduce manual effort — while customers enjoy a seamless, consistent experience across every channel.

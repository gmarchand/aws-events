## Description

Your executive team is asking about agentic AI, quantum computing, and edge systems. Will you be caught on the back-foot, or are you ready with insights and a strategic roadmap? This session moves beyond definitions, offering a systematic framework for technology leaders to proactively evaluate, prioritize, and act on emerging innovations. Learn to build a dynamic technology radar tailored to your business objectives and implement a practical 90-day plan to move from insight to execution. Using real-world examples, this guide will help you shift from a reactive responder to a strategic advisor who anticipates disruption and drives competitive advantage.

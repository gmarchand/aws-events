# AWS re:Invent 2025 - Commonwealth Bank of Australia reimagines modernization via agentic AI (SPS310)

[Video Link](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=H02dc_AV_Vo)

## Description

Commonwealth Bank of Australia (CBA) revolutionizes legacy modernization using AWS Bedrock's agentic AI architecture. Their groundbreaking solution slashes application assessment time from 6 weeks to under 1 hour and reduces overall modernization cycles from 16 weeks to just 1 week. Through specialized AI agents, CBA automatically analyzes legacy code, detects vulnerabilities, and orchestrates complex transformations while maintaining strict banking compliance. Multi-agent workflows handle security assessments, cloud readiness analysis, and automated network flow implementations, enabling transformation of monolithic systems into cloud-native applications with unprecedented speed and precision.

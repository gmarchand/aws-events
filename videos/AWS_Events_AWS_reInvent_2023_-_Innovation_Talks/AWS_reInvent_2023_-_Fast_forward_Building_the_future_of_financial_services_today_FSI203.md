## Description

With AWS, financial institutions are proactively driving change, not just reacting to it. They are modernizing core systems, deriving value from data using analytics and emerging technologies like generative AI, building new products, and evolving the customer experience. In this talk, hear directly from AWS customers, who will share why and how they are using AWS to power the industry’s most mission-critical applications, which must meet the highest standards for security, resiliency, and availability. Learn how financial institutions have built—and continue to build—a stronger, more innovative, and more inclusive industry for consumers and businesses around the world.

## Description

In the rapidly evolving IT landscape, mapping out long-term database changes is challenging. The optimal approach lies in using databases specifically designed for change, agility, and adaptability. In this talk, Jeff Carter, Vice President of Databases and Migrations at AWS, and Rahul Pathak, Vice President of Relational Databases at AWS, address strategies for safeguarding your data against any type of disruption, positioning your organization to easily adopt new technologies, like generative AI, and use near real-time analytics with zero-ETL integrations. AWS databases offer a change-friendly approach that helps you take advantage of innovation. You'll also hear from Heroku on how they are working with AWS to modernize their data offerings in the age of AI.

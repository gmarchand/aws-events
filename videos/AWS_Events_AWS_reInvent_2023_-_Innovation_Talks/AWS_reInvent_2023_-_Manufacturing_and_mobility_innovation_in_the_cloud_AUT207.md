## Description

Industrial companies across automotive, aerospace, consumer electronics, and more are looking to use their data and cloud technologies to reinvent their businesses, optimize operations, accelerate time to market, and generate new revenue streams. Hear from industrial leader Siemens about how they are using their Xcelerator industrial software portfolio powered by AWS to innovate, told through multiple examples. Also hear from Honda about their collaboration with AWS in Japan and North America to accelerate innovation across product development, supply chain, and manufacturing.

## Description

Join AWS Applications VP, Dilip Kumar, for a panel session with C-suite leaders from Asana, DISH, and Woodside Energy on how generative AI is changing the way we think about business outcomes, customer service, and employee happiness. This innovation talk covers the processes and systems that generative AI will transform in organizations and the challenges leaders expect when integrating technology into their tech stack. Hear about new capabilities from the AWS Applications group, including new generative AI features and services.

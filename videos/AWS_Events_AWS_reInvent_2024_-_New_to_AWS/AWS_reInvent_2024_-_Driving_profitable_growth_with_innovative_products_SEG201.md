## Description

In this session, learn how product and technology officers at software companies continue to drive their innovation agenda, maintain differentiation, and avoid disruption. Technologies like generative AI have captured the imagination of the industry and are poised to bring in the next wave of advancements. However, software companies face challenges such as navigating the current macroeconomic climate and optimizing their capital allocation, profitability, and R&D priorities. Explore strategies for how product and technology officers can work together to build a strong foundation for business.
